HR Policy Summary — Enterprise AI Workforce

ANNUAL LEAVE
- Entitlement: 21 calendar days per year (years 1–5); 30 days/year after 5 years of service
- Accrual: 1.75 days per month from day one
- Probation: Leave accrues but cannot be taken during the 6-month probation period
- Carry-forward: Maximum 10 days; forfeited beyond that at year end
- Application: HR Portal → My Leaves → New Request (manager must respond within 48 hours)

SICK LEAVE (UAE Labour Law — Federal Decree No. 33 of 2021)
- First 15 days: Full pay
- Next 30 days: Half pay
- Next 45 days: Unpaid
- Total: 90 days maximum per year
- Requires medical certificate for absences over 2 consecutive days

MATERNITY LEAVE
- Statutory (UAE law): 60 days
- Company-enhanced policy: 90 days full pay
- Flexible hours for first 3 months post-return (core hours must be maintained)

PATERNITY LEAVE
- 10 working days within first 6 months after birth

END OF SERVICE GRATUITY (EOSB)
- Years 1–5: 21 calendar days of basic salary per year
- Beyond 5 years: 30 calendar days of basic salary per year
- Cap: Maximum 2 years' total basic salary
- Resignation < 1 year: No gratuity
- Resignation 1–3 years: One-third of full entitlement
- Resignation 3–5 years: Two-thirds of full entitlement
- Resignation 5+ years: Full entitlement

SALARY & PAYROLL
- Payment: Last working day of each month via WPS (Wage Protection System)
- Payslips: Available on HR Portal by the 25th of each month
- Salary advance: Up to 50% of monthly basic salary; available after 6 months service
