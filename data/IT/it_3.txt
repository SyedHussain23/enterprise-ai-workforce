IT Policy Document

Topic: Device and Equipment Policy

Details:
All company-provided devices must be used responsibly and maintained in good working condition.

DEVICE PROVISIONING:
- Company laptop: Provided on first day of employment (configured and ready)
- Specifications: Based on role requirements (standard/developer/creative)
- Additional equipment: Monitor, keyboard, mouse provided for office workstation
- Mobile phone: Grade 4+ only (see HR benefits policy)
- Access card/badge: Issued by HR on Day 1

DEVICE CONFIGURATION (Pre-configured by IT):
- Operating system: Windows 11 Enterprise / macOS 14 (Sonoma) — fully managed by IT
- Company email configured automatically
- Microsoft 365 / Google Workspace installed
- VPN client (Cisco AnyConnect) pre-installed and configured
- Antivirus/EDR (CrowdStrike Falcon) installed and active
- Full disk encryption enabled (BitLocker/FileVault)
- Mobile Device Management (MDM) agent installed (Intune/Jamf)

USAGE RULES:
- Company devices are for business use only
- Personal use: Limited acceptable personal use permitted during breaks and non-work hours
- Do not use company device for: Personal business ventures, illegal activities, downloading copyrighted content
- Do not allow family members or unauthorized persons to use company device
- Do not disable or bypass any security software installed by IT
- Do not install unauthorized software (see Software Policy)

DEVICE CARE AND MAINTENANCE:
- Keep device clean, free from liquid exposure, and stored securely
- Report any damage immediately to IT (even minor damage — proactive reporting protects you)
- Do not attempt to repair or open the device yourself
- Travel: Use company-provided laptop bag; do not check laptop in airline hold luggage

REPORTING LOST OR STOLEN DEVICE:
1. Report immediately (within 1 hour of discovery) to IT Helpdesk: Ext. 1001
2. IT will remotely wipe device to protect company data
3. File police report if stolen (required for insurance claim)
4. HR and IT will jointly assess incident and determine replacement procedure
5. First theft/loss with police report: Replacement device issued
6. Second loss: Employee may be required to contribute to replacement cost

DEVICE RETURN ON EXIT:
- All company devices must be returned on last working day
- IT conducts device inspection and wipes device upon return
- Outstanding device return: Deducted from final settlement
- Company data may not be transferred to personal devices upon leaving

PERIPHERAL EQUIPMENT:
- External monitors, keyboards, mice: Available on request for office use
- Webcam: Available on request for video conferencing (if not built-in)
- Headsets: Issued to customer-facing roles
- Additional equipment requests: IT Portal → Equipment Request

DEVICE REFRESH CYCLE:
- Laptop replaced every 3 years (subject to condition)
- Urgent replacement: Approved by IT Manager for devices with critical issues
- Loaner devices available (IT storage) for temporary use
