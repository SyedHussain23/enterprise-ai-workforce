IT Policy Document

Topic: Video Conferencing and Collaboration Tools Guide

Details:
Comprehensive guide to using company-approved video conferencing and collaboration tools.

MICROSOFT TEAMS (Primary Internal Tool):
Setup: Pre-installed on all company devices, sign in with company email
Mobile: Download Teams app on personal phone for flexibility

TEAMS KEY FEATURES:
Chat: Direct messages (1:1 or group), file sharing, reactions, voice messages
Calls: Audio and video calls with screen sharing
Meetings: Scheduled with calendar integration, recurring meetings
Channels: Organized spaces for team or project communication
Files: SharePoint integration — files shared in Teams saved to SharePoint automatically
Together Mode: Virtual seating arrangement for large team meetings

ZOOM (External Meetings):
- Company Zoom account: Unlimited meeting duration (personal free accounts: 40-min limit)
- Join: zoom.us/join or Zoom app → Sign In → Company account
- Scheduling: From Outlook → New Event → Add Zoom Meeting
- Waiting Room: Always enable for external participant meetings
- Recording: Ask all participants for consent before recording

VIDEO MEETING BEST PRACTICES:
Before meeting:
- Test audio/video (Zoom: Settings → Test Computer Audio/Video)
- Close unnecessary applications (reduces bandwidth, no notifications popping up)
- Ensure professional background or use virtual background
- Charge laptop if on battery
- Prepare and share agenda 24 hours in advance for meetings > 30 minutes

During meeting:
- Mute when not speaking (especially in large calls)
- Turn camera ON for all team meetings (off only for 30+ person webinars)
- Raise hand feature for large meetings (Teams: ✋ React → Raise Hand)
- Use chat for questions in large meetings to avoid interrupting speaker

After meeting:
- Share meeting notes within 24 hours
- Update task/project board with action items from meeting
- Send recording link if meeting was recorded (Teams: auto-posted in meeting chat)

LARGE SCALE MEETINGS AND WEBINARS:
- Teams Live Events: Up to 10,000 attendees (Town Halls, company-wide updates)
- Zoom Webinar: Up to 500 attendees (training, external presentations)
- Booking required: Submit request via IT Portal → Collaboration → Large Meeting Request

CONNECTIVITY ISSUES DURING MEETINGS:
- Turn off video temporarily to reduce bandwidth
- Switch to phone audio if video not working
- Dial-in number available in every meeting invite
- If still issues: Disconnect VPN temporarily for video call (re-enable after) — with manager permission
