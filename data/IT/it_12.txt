IT Policy Document

Topic: Antivirus, Endpoint Protection, and Malware Response

Details:
The company uses enterprise-grade endpoint protection to defend against malware and cyber threats.

COMPANY ENDPOINT PROTECTION:
- Solution: CrowdStrike Falcon (industry-leading EDR — Endpoint Detection and Response)
- Deployed on: All company Windows, Mac, and Linux devices
- Auto-updates: Threat intelligence updated every 15 minutes from global threat database
- Real-time monitoring: Continuously monitors all processes, files, and network connections
- Behavioral analysis: Detects unknown threats based on behavior, not just known signatures

WHAT ENDPOINT PROTECTION DOES:
- Blocks known malware (viruses, trojans, ransomware, spyware)
- Detects and blocks suspicious behavior (even from new, unknown malware)
- Blocks malicious websites and downloads
- Prevents unauthorized software from running
- Alerts IT Security within seconds of detecting a threat

EMPLOYEE RESPONSIBILITIES:
- Do NOT disable or pause CrowdStrike Falcon — ever
- Do NOT whitelist software without IT approval
- Do NOT attempt to manually remove malware yourself
- Report any unusual pop-ups or security warnings immediately
- Keep devices on and connected to network at least once a week (receives updates)

MALWARE INCIDENT RESPONSE:
If you see unusual pop-ups, system slowing dramatically, or files being encrypted:
1. IMMEDIATELY stop what you're doing
2. Do NOT try to close pop-ups or click anything on screen
3. Unplug network cable (or turn off Wi-Fi) to isolate the device
4. Do NOT turn off the computer (preserves forensic evidence)
5. Call IT Security immediately: Ext. 1001 (24/7)
6. IT will remotely assess and send technician if needed

RANSOMWARE RESPONSE SPECIFICALLY:
- Do NOT pay any ransom demand (company policy, and law enforcement guidance)
- Isolate affected device immediately (unplug from network)
- IT restores from backup (see Backup Policy)
- All ransomware incidents reported to UAE Cybersecurity Council

LAPTOP SCANS:
- Full system scan runs automatically every Sunday at 2 AM
- Quick scan runs daily at 1 PM
- Manual scan available: CrowdStrike icon → Run Scan
- Do not interrupt scans — laptop can be in sleep mode during scan

REMOVABLE MEDIA SCANNING:
- All USB drives automatically scanned when connected to company device
- Infected USB detected: Automatically blocked and IT alerted
- Personal USB drives: Not recommended and monitored (see Cybersecurity Policy)
