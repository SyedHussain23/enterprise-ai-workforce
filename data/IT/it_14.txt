IT Policy Document

Topic: Cloud Services and SaaS Policy

Details:
Employees must only use company-approved cloud services for business data.

APPROVED CLOUD SERVICES:
Productivity: Microsoft 365 (OneDrive, SharePoint, Teams, Exchange Online)
Video Conferencing: Zoom, Microsoft Teams
HR System: Company HR Portal (cloud-hosted)
CRM: Salesforce or HubSpot (if applicable)
Project Management: Jira, Asana, Monday.com (role-specific approvals)
Design: Figma, Adobe Creative Cloud (department-approved)
AI Tools: Microsoft Copilot (approved), ChatGPT Business (approved with restrictions)

PROHIBITED (FOR COMPANY DATA):
- Personal Google Drive, Dropbox, Box, iCloud — for storing work files
- Personal email (Gmail, Yahoo, Hotmail) — for sending company documents
- WeTransfer, Mega, Zippyshare — for sharing company files
- Public ChatGPT (free tier) — for inputting company confidential data
- Unapproved third-party AI tools

REQUESTING NEW CLOUD SERVICES:
1. Submit request: IT Portal → Cloud Services → New SaaS Request
2. IT evaluates: Data security, compliance, vendor security posture, UAE data residency requirements
3. Legal reviews terms of service and data processing agreement
4. Finance approves cost (if applicable)
5. Typical evaluation: 2–4 weeks
6. Approved services added to company SSO portal

AI TOOLS POLICY (ChatGPT, Copilot, etc.):
- Company-approved AI: Microsoft Copilot (integrated with M365) — for employees with license
- ChatGPT/other AI tools: Approved with restrictions — NO inputting of:
  * Client names or confidential information
  * Employee personal data
  * Financial figures or unreleased product information
  * Source code containing proprietary algorithms
- AI output must always be reviewed by a human before use in official documents
- AI does not replace professional judgment or legal/financial advice

DATA RESIDENCY:
- UAE-based company data must be stored in UAE or GCC data centers where possible
- Microsoft 365 UAE region: Data stored in UAE North (Dubai) and UAE West (Abu Dhabi)
- Any service requiring data outside UAE/GCC requires CISO and Legal approval

CLOUD SECURITY RESPONSIBILITIES:
- Company IT secures cloud platforms at the service level
- Employees secure their own accounts (strong password + MFA)
- Shared responsibility model: Cloud provider secures infrastructure; IT secures configuration; employees secure data
