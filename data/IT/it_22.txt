IT Policy Document

Topic: IT Asset Management and Tracking

Details:
All company IT assets are tracked and managed throughout their lifecycle.

ASSET TAGGING:
- Every IT asset has a unique asset tag (sticker with barcode/QR code)
- Do not remove or cover asset tags — they identify your company device
- Asset tags on: Laptops, monitors, phones, tablets, printers, network equipment

ASSET ASSIGNMENT:
- All assets assigned to named employees
- Asset assignment documented in IT Asset Management System (ServiceNow/Snipe-IT)
- Employee acknowledges device assignment by signing Asset Acknowledgement Form (Day 1)
- Asset assigned to you = your responsibility for its care

ANNUAL ASSET AUDIT:
- IT conducts physical asset verification annually (typically November)
- Employees confirm possession of all assigned assets
- Missing assets investigated by IT and HR
- Unaccounted assets: Employee responsible unless theft reported

ASSET TRANSFER:
- If transferring to another department: Submit IT Portal → Asset → Transfer Request
- Asset must be handed to IT for re-imaging before transfer to new user
- Never pass company device directly between employees without IT processing

ASSET DISPOSAL:
- Old/broken assets returned to IT — do not throw in regular bin
- IT ensures secure data wiping (NIST 800-88 standard) before any disposal
- Certified data destruction used for storage media
- Certificate of data destruction provided for compliance records
- E-waste: Disposed of via certified e-waste recycler (ISO 14001 compliant)

MOBILE DEVICE MANAGEMENT (MDM):
- All company mobile phones and tablets enrolled in Microsoft Intune/Jamf
- MDM allows IT to: Push security updates, enforce policies, remotely wipe if lost
- MDM does NOT access personal data on personal devices — only work data partition

IT ASSET REQUEST PROCESS:
New equipment needed:
1. Submit: IT Portal → Assets → Request New Asset
2. Include business justification
3. Manager approval required for items > AED 1,000
4. Finance approval for > AED 5,000
5. Delivery time: Standard stock 1–3 business days; custom orders 2–4 weeks

ACCESSORIES:
- Mouse, keyboard, webcam, headset: Available from IT store
- Request via IT Portal → Assets → Peripheral Request
- Typical turnaround: Next business day
