IT Policy Document

Topic: Email and Communication Policy

Details:
Company email and communication tools must be used professionally and in accordance with this policy.

COMPANY EMAIL:
- All official business communication must use your company email: firstname.lastname@company.com
- Company email is company property — subject to monitoring per IT Monitoring Policy
- Confidential emails must be encrypted (use Encrypt button in Outlook for sensitive information)
- Email auto-replies (out-of-office) should be configured when on leave

EMAIL BEST PRACTICES:
- Subject line: Clear, specific, and descriptive ("Q3 Budget Review — Action Required by 15 Dec")
- Reply All: Only use Reply All when response is relevant to ALL recipients
- CC vs BCC: CC = visible, BCC = hidden. Use BCC for large distributions to protect recipient privacy
- Attachments: Maximum 25 MB per email (use SharePoint link for larger files)
- Response time expectation: Within 4 business hours for internal, 1 business day for external

EMAIL PROHIBITED USES:
- Sending chain letters, mass forwards, or spam
- Using company email to sign up for personal services or newsletters
- Sending confidential company data to personal email addresses
- Forwarding business email to personal Gmail/Yahoo/Hotmail
- Sending harassing, discriminatory, or offensive content
- Mass marketing emails without Marketing team approval

MICROSOFT TEAMS (Internal Communication):
- Preferred tool for internal messaging and collaboration
- Channels: Department channels, project channels, company-wide announcements
- Status: Set your availability status accurately (Available/Busy/Do Not Disturb/Away)
- Response time: Within 30 minutes during business hours for direct messages
- Teams for calls: Preferred for ad-hoc calls, reduces email overload

ZOOM (External Meetings):
- Use Zoom for external client and partner meetings
- Always use company Zoom account (not personal)
- Enable waiting room for all client calls
- Record only with all participants' consent (announce at meeting start)

VIDEO MEETING ETIQUETTE:
- Camera ON for internal team meetings (unless explicitly exempt)
- Professional background or use virtual background
- Mute when not speaking in large calls
- Test audio/video before important external meetings

CONFIDENTIAL COMMUNICATIONS:
- Client contracts, financial information, personal employee data: Use encrypted email
- Select "Encrypt" in Outlook before sending
- Do not discuss highly confidential matters in open-plan office areas
- Use meeting rooms for sensitive conversations

WHATSAPP AND PERSONAL MESSAGING:
- Business communication should not rely on personal WhatsApp
- Company WhatsApp Business: Approved for specific customer-facing roles only
- Do not share confidential company information on personal WhatsApp groups
- Client communication on personal WhatsApp requires manager approval
