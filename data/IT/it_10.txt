IT Policy Document

Topic: IT Monitoring and Employee Privacy

Details:
The company monitors IT systems to protect company assets, ensure compliance, and detect security incidents.

WHAT IS MONITORED:
1. Network traffic: All data flowing through company network (for malware detection, DLP)
2. Email: Automated scanning for malware, phishing, and data loss prevention
3. Web browsing: Websites visited on company network or VPN (category-level, not specific page content)
4. Endpoint activity: Application usage, file operations on company devices (for security, not micromanagement)
5. Login activity: All login events, failed logins, access to sensitive systems logged
6. Video surveillance: Physical office areas (common areas, server rooms) — not in private areas

WHAT IS NOT MONITORED:
- Personal devices on Guest Wi-Fi (no company MDM)
- Personal email accounts accessed via personal device
- Personal phone calls
- Physical movements within office (beyond CCTV in common areas)

EMPLOYEE PRIVACY:
- Monitoring is for security and business purposes, not to surveil employees' personal communications
- Company does not monitor personal messages on Teams/Slack beyond automated policy scanning
- IT staff do not access email content without documented business or legal reason
- All monitoring activities logged with audit trail

ACCEPTABLE USE OF MONITORING:
Monitoring data accessed by IT Security only for:
- Investigating security incidents
- Responding to legal or regulatory requirements
- Investigating policy violations (only with HR and legal approval)

PURPOSE OF MONITORING:
- Detect and prevent cyber attacks
- Prevent unauthorized data exfiltration
- Ensure compliance with UAE data protection laws
- Investigate security incidents when they occur
- Provide evidence in case of legal disputes

TRANSPARENCY:
- Employees are informed of monitoring through this policy and acceptable use agreement (signed on joining)
- Specific monitoring actions (e.g., reviewing an employee's email) require manager + HR + IT Security sign-off
- Employees may request information on what data is held about them (Data Subject Access Request)

ON-CALL IT SUPPORT AND MONITORING:
- IT monitors critical systems 24/7 via automated tools and Security Operations Center (SOC)
- Automated alerts for: Unusual login times, large data transfers, malware detection
- IT will contact you if unusual activity is detected on your account
