IT Policy Document

Topic: Password Security Policy

Details:
Strong passwords are the first line of defense against unauthorized access to company systems.

PASSWORD REQUIREMENTS:
- Minimum length: 12 characters (recommended: 16+)
- Must contain all four: uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, special characters (!@#$%^&*)
- Must NOT contain: Your name, username, company name, or common words (password123, qwerty, etc.)
- Must NOT be the same as any of your last 12 passwords
- Must NOT be reused across multiple systems

PASSWORD EXPIRY AND ROTATION:
- Company systems: Password expires every 90 days
- Privileged/admin accounts: Password expires every 60 days
- You will receive reminder notifications 14 days, 7 days, and 1 day before expiry
- Failure to update password before expiry: Account automatically locked (requires IT to unlock)

HOW TO CHANGE YOUR PASSWORD:
Method 1 (Windows): Press Ctrl+Alt+Del → Change Password
Method 2 (HR/IT Portal): Settings → Security → Change Password
Method 3 (Self-service Reset): https://passwordreset.company.internal (requires mobile phone verification)
Note: You can change password at any time, not just when it expires

PASSWORD RESET PROCESS:
If you forgot your password:
1. Go to: https://passwordreset.company.internal
2. Enter your company email address
3. Verify identity via mobile OTP (registered number) or security questions
4. Create new password meeting all requirements
5. If locked out without access to phone: Call IT Helpdesk: Ext. 1001

MULTI-FACTOR AUTHENTICATION (MFA):
- Mandatory for: All company email accounts, VPN access, cloud applications, HR Portal
- Methods accepted: Microsoft Authenticator app (preferred), SMS OTP, Hardware token
- Setup: IT Portal → My Account → Enable MFA
- Lost MFA device: Contact IT Helpdesk immediately for emergency bypass code

PASSWORD MANAGER:
- Company provides: LastPass Enterprise or Bitwarden Business license to all employees
- Use password manager to generate and store unique passwords for each system
- Never write passwords on sticky notes or store in plain text files

SHARING PASSWORDS — STRICTLY PROHIBITED:
- Never share your password with anyone — including IT staff
- IT will NEVER ask for your password via email or phone
- If someone asks for your password: Report it immediately to it-security@company.com
- Violations: Disciplinary action up to termination

SUSPICIOUS ACTIVITY:
- Receive unexpected password reset email? Do NOT click links — call IT immediately
- Notice unusual logins? Report to: it-security@company.com
- Password breach alert from company system: Change password immediately
