IT Policy Document

Topic: Printing, Scanning, and Copier Policy

Details:
Guidelines for using shared printing and scanning equipment in the company.

PRINTER SETUP:
- Office printers automatically configured on company devices on first login
- If printer not visible: IT Portal → Printer Setup → Auto-Add
- Home printing for WFH: Personal printer, company covers up to 50 A4 pages/month (claim via Finance portal)

SECURE PRINTING (Mandatory for Confidential Documents):
- Swipe badge or enter PIN at printer to release print job
- Print job held in queue for 4 hours — auto-deleted if not collected
- Never send confidential document to open printer without secure print enabled
- Enable secure print: In print dialog → Properties → Secure Print → Enter 4-digit PIN

PRINTER LOCATIONS:
- Floor 1 (Main): 2× A4 color, 1× A3 plotter
- Floor 2: 2× A4 black & white, 1× A4 color
- Floor 3: 1× A4 black & white (IT department)
- Server Room: 1× Label printer (IT only)

SCANNING DOCUMENTS:
- Scan to your email: Press Scan → Email → Enter your company email
- Scan to SharePoint: Scan → SharePoint → Select your folder
- Maximum scan resolution for email: 300 DPI (higher quality: save to SharePoint or USB)
- Scanning personal documents: Limited use acceptable during breaks

PRINTING ALLOWANCE AND COST AWARENESS:
- Monthly print report sent to managers showing department usage
- Company actively promotes paperless practices — use digital documents whenever possible
- Double-sided printing enabled by default (saves paper)
- Color printing: Use only when necessary (3x cost of black and white)

CONFIDENTIAL DOCUMENT HANDLING:
- Printed confidential documents: Collect immediately from printer
- Do not leave prints on printer tray
- Shred all printed confidential documents — shredder located at each printer station
- External shredding service: For bulk shredding — contact Facilities team

PAPER AND CONSUMABLES:
- Paper refill: Facilities team restocks printers (request via FM Portal if urgent)
- Toner/ink low alert: Printer displays message — do NOT open printer yourself; submit IT ticket
- Paper jams: Follow on-screen printer guide, or call IT Ext. 1001
