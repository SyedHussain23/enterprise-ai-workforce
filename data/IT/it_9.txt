IT Policy Document

Topic: BYOD (Bring Your Own Device) Policy

Details:
Employees wishing to use personal devices to access company resources must follow this policy.

WHAT IS BYOD:
Using a personal (non-company-issued) device — laptop, phone, tablet — to access company email, applications, or data.

BYOD ELIGIBILITY:
- Available to employees Grade 3+ who need occasional remote access
- Personal phones: Any employee may use personal phone for company email and Teams
- Personal laptops: Must be approved by IT before accessing company systems

BYOD REGISTRATION:
1. Submit BYOD registration request: IT Portal → BYOD → Register Device
2. Device details required: Make, model, operating system version, serial number
3. IT evaluates device security posture:
   - OS must be current and supported (no Windows 7, iOS < 16, Android < 11)
   - Device must have a PIN/password lock
   - No jailbreak/root on mobile devices
4. IT installs Mobile Device Management (MDM) profile on approved device
5. Registration completed within 3 business days

MDM ON PERSONAL DEVICES:
- For phones/tablets: Microsoft Intune app installed — manages ONLY work-related data partition
- Intune does NOT access personal photos, messages, or apps
- Work apps and data isolated in a separate encrypted container (Microsoft Enterprise Mobility)
- If you leave company: Work container wiped remotely (personal data unaffected)

WHAT YOU CAN DO ON PERSONAL DEVICES (Once registered):
- Access company email via Outlook for iOS/Android
- Join Teams meetings and use Teams messaging
- Access SharePoint/OneDrive via official apps
- Use approved company applications

WHAT YOU CANNOT DO ON PERSONAL DEVICES:
- Access company databases or sensitive systems (Finance, HR admin, code repositories) from personal device
- Download or locally store company confidential data
- Use personal device to process payment card data (PCI compliance)

SECURITY REQUIREMENTS FOR PERSONAL DEVICES:
- Must have screen lock (PIN, pattern, biometric) — minimum 6-digit PIN
- Auto-lock after maximum 5 minutes of inactivity
- Must keep OS updated to latest supported version
- Cannot be jailbroken or rooted
- Must have antivirus app (company recommends: Lookout or Norton Mobile)

PERSONAL DEVICE LOST OR STOLEN:
- Report immediately to IT: Ext. 1001
- IT will remotely wipe the work container only (personal data retained)
- File police report for stolen device

COST:
- BYOD: Employee bears cost of personal device and data plan
- Company does NOT reimburse personal device costs for BYOD users
- Exception: Grade 5+ employees on fully remote contracts may receive device stipend
