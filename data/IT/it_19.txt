IT Policy Document

Topic: Acceptable Use Policy for IT Resources

Details:
This policy defines what constitutes acceptable and unacceptable use of all company IT resources.

COMPANY IT RESOURCES COVERED:
- Company laptops, desktops, phones, tablets
- Company network (wired and wireless)
- Company email and communication tools
- Company applications and software
- Internet access via company network or VPN
- Company-owned cloud storage and collaboration tools

ACCEPTABLE PERSONAL USE (Limited):
The company allows limited personal use of IT resources during breaks and non-work hours:
- Checking personal email occasionally
- General web browsing (non-work related) on breaks
- Personal calls or messages during lunch break
- Light personal social media use outside working hours
Personal use must not: Impact productivity, consume excessive bandwidth, violate any other policy

UNACCEPTABLE AND PROHIBITED USE:
1. Using company IT resources for personal business or commercial activities
2. Accessing, downloading, or sharing illegal content (pirated software, movies, etc.)
3. Downloading or distributing copyrighted material without authorization
4. Visiting adult, gambling, or other inappropriate websites
5. Sending harassing, threatening, or discriminatory communications
6. Installing unauthorized software or modifying system settings
7. Sharing company credentials or allowing others to use your accounts
8. Attempting to access systems or data without authorization
9. Using company resources to compete with the employer
10. Using IT resources to conduct activities that violate UAE law

SOCIAL MEDIA ON COMPANY DEVICES:
- Personal social media use: Permitted during breaks and lunch (limited)
- Do NOT post confidential company information on any social media platform
- Do NOT conduct personal social media business using company bandwidth excessively
- Business social media (LinkedIn, company accounts): During work hours as appropriate

MONITORING AND PRIVACY:
- Company reserves right to monitor all activity on company devices and network
- Employees have no expectation of privacy for communications on company systems
- Monitoring activities detailed in IT Monitoring Policy (IT_10)

CONSEQUENCES OF VIOLATIONS:
- First violation: Warning and mandatory security training
- Repeated violations: Formal disciplinary action
- Serious violations (illegal activities): Immediate termination and potential legal action

HARDWARE MODIFICATIONS:
- Do not add, remove, or modify hardware components
- Do not install RAM, hard drives, peripherals, or cards without IT authorization
- Do not apply stickers or markings that obscure asset tags

By using company IT resources, all employees acknowledge acceptance of this policy.
