IT Policy Document

Topic: Email Account Setup and Microsoft 365 Guide

Details:
Step-by-step guide to setting up and using your company email and Microsoft 365 applications.

EMAIL ACCOUNT SETUP:
Your company email: firstname.lastname@company.com
Initial password: Set by IT on Day 1 (you must change immediately)
Email client: Microsoft Outlook (primary), Outlook Web App (browser-based backup)

FIRST LOGIN:
1. Open Outlook on your company laptop
2. Enter your company email address
3. Enter temporary password (provided by IT on Day 1)
4. You will be prompted to change password immediately
5. Set up MFA (see MFA Policy) — required before full access
6. Configure Outlook signature (template provided by HR/Marketing)

ACCESSING EMAIL FROM ANYWHERE:
- Laptop: Outlook desktop app
- Web browser: https://outlook.company.com (or portal.office.com)
- Phone: Download Outlook app for iOS/Android, sign in with company email

MICROSOFT 365 APPLICATIONS INCLUDED:
- Outlook: Email and calendar
- Word, Excel, PowerPoint: Document creation
- Teams: Chat, calls, video meetings, file sharing
- OneDrive: 1 TB personal cloud storage for work files
- SharePoint: Team and department shared files
- OneNote: Digital notebook
- Forms: Surveys and quizzes
- Planner: Task management
- Power BI: Data visualization (specific roles)
- Copilot AI: AI assistant integrated across M365 apps (licensed users)

OUTLOOK TIPS FOR PRODUCTIVITY:
- Focused Inbox: Separates important emails from newsletters/notifications
- Rules: Automatically sort emails to folders (Home → Rules → Create Rule)
- Quick Steps: One-click actions for common email workflows
- Calendar: Schedule meetings with Teams link (New Meeting → Teams Meeting)
- Shared Calendars: View team members' availability for scheduling

TEAMS GUIDE:
- Direct messages: Find colleague by name, click chat icon
- Team channels: Organized by department/project for group communication
- @Mention: Tag colleague to get their attention in a channel (@firstname)
- File sharing: Drag and drop files into Teams chat or channel
- Tabs: Add SharePoint, Planner, or apps directly in a channel

STORAGE AND FILE MANAGEMENT:
- OneDrive: Your personal work files (1 TB)
- SharePoint: Shared team files and documents
- Never save to: Local C: drive only (not backed up)
- Always sync OneDrive on new device: Open OneDrive app → sign in with company account

OUTLOOK CALENDAR BEST PRACTICES:
- Mark working hours in calendar (Options → Calendar → Work Hours)
- Block focus time: Schedule 2-hour focus blocks to minimize meeting interruptions
- Meeting duration: Default to 25 or 50 minutes (leave 5/10 min buffer)
