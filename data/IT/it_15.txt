Topic: IT Help Desk — Support and Ticketing Guide

Details:
The IT Help Desk provides technical support to all employees. Here's how to get the fastest and most effective help.

CONTACTING IT SUPPORT:
Option 1 — IT Portal (Recommended): https://it.company.internal
Option 2 — Email: it-support@company.com
Option 3 — Phone: Internal Ext. 1001 | External: +971-4-XXX-1001
Option 4 — Walk-in: IT Help Desk, Building A, Ground Floor, Room G-04
Support Hours: Sunday–Thursday, 7:30 AM–7:00 PM
After-hours emergency: +971-55-XXX-XXXX (critical business systems only)

PRIORITY LEVELS AND RESPONSE TIMES:
Critical (P1): System down affecting multiple users or business-critical operations
- Response: 30 minutes | Resolution target: 4 hours
- Examples: Email server down, VPN not working for all users, major application outage

High (P2): System down affecting one user, significant productivity impact
- Response: 1 hour | Resolution target: 8 hours
- Examples: Laptop won't start, cannot access email, printer completely failed

Medium (P3): Issue causing inconvenience but workaround exists
- Response: 4 hours | Resolution target: 2 business days
- Examples: Software slow, one application not working, non-critical printer issue

Low (P4): General questions, training requests, non-urgent items
- Response: 1 business day | Resolution target: 5 business days
- Examples: Software installation request, general IT questions, how-to guidance

HOW TO SUBMIT A GOOD TICKET:
Include in your ticket:
- Your name, department, location (office, WFH)
- Description of the problem (what you were doing when it happened)
- Error message (exact text or screenshot)
- When did it start?
- Have you tried any solutions already?
- How urgent is it (impact on your work)?

COMMON ISSUES AND QUICK FIXES:
Cannot connect to Wi-Fi: Forget network and reconnect, restart Wi-Fi adapter (Device Manager)
Computer running slow: Restart computer, check if Windows update pending
Outlook not opening: Restart Outlook in safe mode (Ctrl while clicking Outlook icon)
Teams not working: Sign out and back in, check internet connection
Cannot print: Restart print spooler (Services.msc → Print Spooler → Restart)
External monitor not detected: Check cable, press Win+P → Duplicate/Extend

ESCALATION PROCESS:
If ticket not resolved within stated time: IT Manager automatically notified
Further escalation: Contact IT Manager directly at it-manager@company.com

SATISFACTION FEEDBACK:
After ticket resolution: Survey sent automatically via email
Your feedback helps IT improve service quality
Overall satisfaction target: 90%+
