Company Policy Document

Topic: Office Facilities, Amenities & Workplace Services

Details:
Available office facilities, how to access them, and usage guidelines for all employees.

OFFICE LOCATIONS:
- Headquarters: Dubai (primary office — all departments)
- Branch offices: Abu Dhabi, Sharjah (department heads confirm which teams are based where)
- Office hours: Sunday–Thursday, 8:30 AM–6:30 PM (access card required outside these hours)

OFFICE FACILITIES OVERVIEW:

Meeting Rooms:
- Available on all floors; book via Outlook Calendar → Room Finder
- Capacity: 4-person huddle rooms, 8-person boardrooms, 20-person conference rooms
- AV equipment: Screen, HDMI, webcam, microphone installed in all rooms
- Problem with AV equipment: Contact IT via Ext. 1001 or it-support@company.com
- Etiquette: Book only what you need; cancel if unused; leave room clean and tidy

Kitchen & Pantry:
- Fully equipped kitchen on each floor: Coffee machine, microwave, refrigerator, toaster, water dispenser
- Keep refrigerator clean — items without names cleared every Friday
- Dishes must be washed immediately after use (dishwasher available)
- Halal food is available in canteen; labels confirm Halal status
- Prayer room: Dedicated, clean, and private prayer room on each floor

Reception & Visitor Management:
- All visitors must register at reception; host employee must collect their guest
- Visitor badges issued at reception and returned upon departure
- Couriers and deliveries: Managed by reception; notify reception of expected deliveries
- Access cards: Provided on Day 1; lost cards reported to Facilities within 1 hour

Car Parking:
- Allocated parking for senior staff (Grade 6+): Contact Facilities
- Visitor parking: Ground floor, marked zones
- Motorbike parking: Designated area at basement level
- Carpooling encouraged — contact HR for carpooling partner matching

PRINTER & STATIONERY:
- Printers: Available on each floor; use secure print (PIN required) for confidential documents
- Stationery requests: Submit via Facilities Portal → Stationery Request
- Personal printing (CVs, personal documents): Not permitted on company printers
- Scanning to email: Available on all multifunction printers

GYM & WELLNESS:
- Gym subsidy: AED 300/month reimbursable via Finance Portal → Allowances → Gym (submit gym receipt)
- In-office shower facilities available in main HQ building (ground floor)
- Wellness room: Available for prayer, meditation, or quiet time (no booking required)

CANTEEN & FOOD:
- Staff canteen: Hot meals available Sunday–Thursday 12:00 PM–2:00 PM
- Subsidised meals: AED 15–25 per meal (charged to employee meal account / deducted monthly)
- Vending machines: Available 24/7 on all floors
- External delivery (Talabat, Deliveroo): Permitted; collect at reception; do not block workspaces
- Ramadan: Canteen hours adjusted; food served after Iftar time

ACCESSIBILITY:
- All floors accessible by elevator; wheelchair access to all facilities
- Accessible parking bays at ground level
- Large-print signs available on request from Facilities

IT & TECHNICAL SUPPORT ON-SITE:
- IT helpdesk: Ground floor, HQ; also available remotely via Ext. 1001
- Loaner equipment: Available from IT for temporary needs (charger, keyboard, mouse)
- Phone system: Internal extensions listed on SharePoint → Telephone Directory

FACILITIES REQUESTS:
- Maintenance issues (AC, lighting, furniture, cleaning): Submit via Facilities Portal or Ext. 2000
- Response time: Same day for urgent; 3 business days for routine
- Office furniture requests: Ergonomic chairs, standing desks, monitor stands available on request

CONTACTS:
- Facilities Team: facilities@company.com | Ext. 2000
- IT Helpdesk: it-support@company.com | Ext. 1001
- Reception: reception@company.com | Ext. 0
- Canteen: canteen@company.com
