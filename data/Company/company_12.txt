Company Policy Document

Topic: Diversity, Equity & Inclusion (DEI) Policy

Details:
Our commitment to building a diverse, equitable, and inclusive workplace in the UAE.

OUR DEI COMMITMENT:
We are proud to be a diverse organisation with employees from 40+ nationalities. We believe that diverse teams are more innovative, more effective, and deliver better results for our clients. Inclusion is not just a policy — it is how we operate every day.

KEY DEI PILLARS:

1. EMIRATIZATION:
- We are committed to the UAE government's Emiratization mandate
- Targets set annually in line with NAFIS programme requirements
- Dedicated UAE National Development Programme: Accelerated career tracks, mentorship by senior leaders, specialised training
- Quarterly reporting to MOHRE on Emiratization progress
- UAE National graduates: Fast-track recruitment programme (applications via career page)

2. GENDER BALANCE:
- Target: 40% women in management roles by 2027
- Equal pay for equal work: Annual pay equity audit conducted by Finance/HR
- Parental leave: 90 days maternity + 10 days paternity (company-enhanced, above UAE law)
- No gender-based promotion barriers — promotion criteria are transparent and objective
- Women in Leadership mentoring programme: Open to all female employees Grade 3+

3. NATIONALITY & CULTURAL DIVERSITY:
- 40+ nationalities represented in our workforce
- Multicultural calendar: We celebrate Eid, Diwali, Christmas, Chinese New Year, and other key cultural events
- No language discrimination: Formal communications in English (internal); Arabic for government/client relations
- National dress is welcomed for all nationalities
- No discrimination based on nationality, ethnicity, or country of origin — UAE Labour Law Article 4

4. PEOPLE OF DETERMINATION (DISABILITY INCLUSION):
- We comply with UAE's Cabinet Resolution No. 43 of 2021 on employing people of determination
- Accessibility: Offices designed for wheelchair access; accessible bathrooms on all floors
- Reasonable adjustments made for employees with disabilities (remote work, flexible hours, equipment)
- Dedicated HR contact for disability-related support: hr@company.com

5. RELIGION & BELIEF:
- Respect for all religious practices
- Prayer room available in all offices (dedicated, clean, private)
- Flexible prayer timings accommodated within work hours
- Ramadan working hours reduced for all employees (6 hours/day) — see Company_1
- Religious holidays: UAE public holidays cover major Islamic holidays; non-Muslim employees may take personal leave for their religious observances

6. AGE & EXPERIENCE:
- We value both young talent and experienced professionals
- Reverse mentoring programme: Junior employees coach senior leaders on technology and new trends
- No mandatory retirement age in the UAE private sector

ANTI-DISCRIMINATION POLICY:
We prohibit discrimination based on:
- Race, nationality, colour, or ethnicity
- Gender, gender identity, or gender expression
- Religion or belief
- Age (in compliance with UAE law)
- Disability or health condition
- Marital or family status
- Pregnancy or maternity

REPORTING DISCRIMINATION:
- Report to: hr@company.com or Ethics Hotline +971-4-XXX-9999
- Anonymous reporting available
- All complaints investigated within 10 business days
- Confirmed discrimination: Disciplinary action up to termination

- HR Department: hr@company.com
- MOHRE Emiratization queries: mohre@company.com
