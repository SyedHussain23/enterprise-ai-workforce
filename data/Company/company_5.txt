Company Policy Document

Topic: UAE Public Holidays & Company-Observed Holidays

Details:
Official public holidays in the UAE and additional company-observed days off.

UAE OFFICIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAYS (as declared by UAE Federal Government annually):
1. New Year's Day — 1 January
2. Eid Al Fitr — 3 days (dates vary by moon sighting — typically end of Ramadan)
3. Arafat Day (Eid Al Adha Eve) — 1 day
4. Eid Al Adha — 3 days (dates vary by Islamic calendar)
5. Islamic New Year (Al Hijri New Year) — 1 day
6. Prophet Mohammed's Birthday (Al Mawlid Al Nabawi) — 1 day
7. Commemoration Day (Martyr's Day) — 30 November
8. UAE National Day — 2–3 December (2 days)

TOTAL UAE PUBLIC HOLIDAYS: Approximately 14–15 days per year (exact dates announced by government)

COMPANY-ADDITIONAL HOLIDAYS:
- The company may declare additional bridge days around public holidays (announced with at least 1-week notice)
- Easter Monday: Additional day off (for applicable offices/nationalities)

HOW HOLIDAYS WORK:
- All employees receive full pay on public holidays
- If a public holiday falls on your weekend (Friday/Saturday): No substitute day is given (UAE practice)
- If a public holiday falls during your annual leave: The holiday is not counted as leave — leave days are refunded
- Public holidays during sick leave: Sick leave days are not refunded for public holidays

WORKING ON PUBLIC HOLIDAYS:
- Operational roles (customer service, security, maintenance, IT on-call): May be required to work
- Compensation for working on public holiday: 150% of daily rate + compensatory day off within 90 days
- Must be approved in advance by department head + HR

RAMADAN:
- Working hours reduced to 6 hours/day for ALL employees during Ramadan (as per UAE law)
- Eating, drinking, and smoking in public/shared areas is prohibited during fasting hours
- Respect fasting colleagues — avoid eating at desks in open-plan offices
- Ramadan dates confirmed by HR in advance (moon sighting dependent — announced nationally)

EID AL FITR & EID AL ADHA:
- 3 days official + company may add bridge day if adjacent to weekend
- Bonus (Eid Bonus): AED 500 equivalent gift voucher or bonus for all employees (announced separately)

NATIONAL DAY (2-3 DECEMBER):
- All employees celebrate UAE National Day
- Employees encouraged to wear UAE colours (red, green, white, black) on National Day
- Office decorations and celebrations organised by the Culture Committee

HOLIDAY CALENDAR:
- Annual holiday calendar published by HR by December 31st for the following year
- Available on: HR Portal → Documents → Annual Holiday Calendar

CONTACTS:
- HR Portal: https://hr.company.internal
- Holiday queries: hr@company.com

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS:
Q: When are Eid dates announced?
A: After official UAE moon sighting announcement. HR communicates within 24 hours of official announcement.

Q: If Eid falls during my annual leave, do I get those days back?
A: Yes — public holidays during approved annual leave are refunded automatically.

Q: Do I get a day off if I work on National Day?
A: Unless you're in an operational role, National Day is a day off. If you must work, you receive a compensatory day.
