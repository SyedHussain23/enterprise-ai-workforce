Company Policy Document

Topic: Career Growth, Promotion & Internal Mobility

Details:
How employees can grow their careers within the company through promotions, transfers, and development programmes.

CAREER FRAMEWORK:
The company uses an 8-grade career framework:
- Grade 1–2: Entry level / Graduate
- Grade 3–4: Professional / Specialist
- Grade 5–6: Senior Professional / Team Lead
- Grade 7: Manager / Senior Manager
- Grade 8: Director / VP / C-Suite

Each grade has a defined salary band, KPI profile, and behavioural expectations documented in the Role Profile Library (SharePoint → HR → Role Profiles).

PROMOTION POLICY:

Eligibility for Promotion:
- Minimum 12 months in current role (unless exceptional fast-track case approved by CHRO)
- Performance rating of 4 (Exceeds Expectations) or 5 (Outstanding) in last review cycle
- No active disciplinary warning on file
- Demonstrating capability at the next grade (not just mastery of current grade)

Promotion Process:
1. Manager identifies high-potential employee and initiates promotion case
2. HR and Department Head review against grade criteria
3. Salary Calibration: Finance ensures new salary is within band and budget
4. CHRO approval for Grade 6+ promotions
5. Employee informed in writing; promotion effective next payroll cycle
6. Announcement made to team (with employee consent)

Promotion Salary Increase:
- Within-grade salary band adjustment: Minimum 15% increase upon promotion
- Maximum: Not exceeding top of new grade band

FAST-TRACK PROGRAMME:
- Top 10% performers identified through calibration process
- Eligible for promotion in under 12 months with CHRO approval
- Assigned Executive Sponsor from leadership team
- Included in High-Potential (HiPo) development programme

INTERNAL TRANSFERS:
- Employees can apply for internal openings after 12 months in current role (6 months for probation completion)
- Internal job board: HR Portal → Careers → Internal Openings
- Process: Express interest → Current manager informed (after application submitted) → Interview → Offer
- Lateral transfers (same grade): Supported with manager approval; no salary change required unless justified
- International transfers: Require CHRO approval + legal/visa review

CAREER DEVELOPMENT PROGRAMMES:

Management Development Programme (MDP):
- For employees targeting Grade 7+ (Manager level)
- 6-month intensive: Leadership modules, project sponsorship, mentoring from C-suite
- Sponsored by the company — no cost to employee
- Nomination by HR + Department Head

Graduate Development Programme (GDP):
- For fresh graduates (Grade 1–2); 2-year rotational programme
- Rotations across 3 departments; mentored by senior employee
- Targeted for Emiratization (preference given to UAE Nationals but open to all)

Mentoring Programme:
- Formal mentoring: 6-month pairing with senior leader; structured sessions monthly
- Sign up: HR Portal → Learning → Mentoring Programme → Apply as Mentee
- Open to all employees from Grade 3+

SUCCESSION PLANNING:
- All Grade 6+ positions have identified successors (ready now, ready in 1 year, ready in 2–3 years)
- Successors receive targeted development to fill gaps
- Succession plans reviewed annually by HR and leadership

CONTACTS:

- Mentoring programme: learning@company.com
- High-potential programme: hipo@company.com
