Company Policy Document

Topic: Company Culture, Values & Working Environment

Details:
Our organisational culture, values, and the environment we strive to build every day.

OUR COMPANY CULTURE:
We are a people-first organisation operating in the UAE/GCC region with a diverse team of 40+ nationalities. Our culture is built on mutual respect, high performance, and inclusive collaboration.

CORE VALUES:
1. People First — Employees are our greatest asset. We invest in their growth and wellbeing.
2. Customer Excellence — We deliver exceptional value to every client, every time.
3. Integrity Always — We do the right thing, even when no one is watching.
4. Innovation — We challenge the status quo and embrace better ways of working.
5. Inclusivity — We celebrate diversity and ensure every voice is heard.
6. One Team — We win together, learn together, and grow together.

CULTURE PILLARS:

High Performance:
- We set clear goals and measure outcomes, not activity
- Excellence is expected and mediocrity is not accepted
- Regular feedback keeps everyone aligned and improving
- High performers are rewarded through bonuses, promotions, and recognition

Psychological Safety:
- Employees feel safe to speak up, share ideas, raise concerns, and make mistakes
- Leaders model vulnerability and open communication
- No blame culture — we learn from failure collectively

Diversity & Inclusion:
- 40+ nationalities represented in our workforce
- We celebrate UAE National Day, Eid, Diwali, Christmas, and other cultural events
- Emiratization is a key strategic goal — we actively hire and develop UAE Nationals
- Gender balance targets are set and tracked at leadership levels

Continuous Learning:
- Annual learning budget: AED 5,000–15,000 per employee (grade-dependent)
- Monthly lunch & learn sessions (all-hands)
- Access to LinkedIn Learning, Udemy, and professional certifications
- Internal knowledge-sharing Fridays (virtual, every first Sunday)

Work-Life Integration:
- Flexible working hours (core hours 10 AM–4 PM)
- WFH: 2 days per week for eligible employees
- Mental health and wellbeing support via our EAP (6 free sessions)
- Gym subsidy: AED 300/month

HOW WE WORK TOGETHER:
- Open-door policy: All managers and leaders are approachable — no hierarchy barriers
- Town Halls: Quarterly all-hands meetings with CEO and leadership team
- Skip-level meetings: Available on request — speak directly with senior leaders
- Culture Committee: Employee-driven group that plans events, celebrates milestones, and drives culture initiatives

CELEBRATING SUCCESS:
- Employee of the Month / Quarter: Nominated by peers, recognised with certificate + AED 500 voucher
- Team celebrations: Budget provided per department for team milestones
- Spot bonuses: Managers can award up to AED 1,000 for outstanding performance
- LinkedIn recommendations from leadership for exceptional work

WHAT WE EXPECT FROM LEADERS:
- Lead by example — embody our values visibly
- Hold regular 1-on-1s with direct reports (minimum bi-weekly)
- Provide honest, timely feedback — both positive and developmental
- Remove obstacles for their teams — servant leadership mindset
- Develop successors — great managers build great managers
