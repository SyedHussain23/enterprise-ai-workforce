Company Policy Document

Topic: Workplace Inclusion, Belonging & Equal Opportunity

Details:
Creating a workplace where every employee feels they belong and has equal opportunity to succeed.

INCLUSION PHILOSOPHY:
Diversity is being invited to the party. Inclusion is being asked to dance.
We are committed to ensuring every single employee — regardless of background — feels valued, heard, and empowered to contribute fully.

EQUAL OPPORTUNITY:
- All recruitment, promotion, and development decisions are based solely on merit and capability
- Job descriptions reviewed annually to remove biased language
- Hiring panels are diverse — no single-gender or single-nationality panels
- Salary bands are transparent within each grade — no unexplained pay gaps

CREATING A BELONGING CULTURE:

Onboarding for Inclusion:
- All new employees complete DEI awareness module in Week 1 (30 minutes online, mandatory)
- Buddy programme: New joiner paired with a buddy from a different nationality/department
- "Culture introduction" session conducted by Culture Committee in Week 2

Inclusive Meetings:
- Meetings should not be held on Friday afternoon (Islamic Jumu'ah prayer time)
- All voices must be heard — facilitator responsibility to invite quieter participants to share
- Meeting materials shared in advance to support non-native English speakers
- No jargon or acronyms without explanation — inclusivity of language is essential

Inclusive Language:
- Use gender-neutral language where possible (e.g., "team" not "guys")
- Avoid idioms or cultural references that may exclude non-native speakers
- Names: Make effort to pronounce colleagues' names correctly — it matters
- Disability language: Use person-first language ("person with a disability" not "disabled person")

EMPLOYEE RESOURCE GROUPS (ERGs):
- Women in Leadership Network: Monthly sessions, mentoring, networking
- Young Professionals Network: For employees under 30; skill-building events
- UAE Nationals (Emiratization) Network: Career development focus, senior mentorship
- Wellness & Wellbeing Group: Organises health, fitness, and mental health events

INCLUSIVE CELEBRATIONS:
- All major cultural and religious events acknowledged in company calendar
- Company does not impose one culture's celebrations on all; all celebrations are optional
- Eid Gatherings, Diwali sweets, Christmas lunches, Chinese New Year — all welcomed
- Iftar dinner: Company-organised during Ramadan for all staff

ACCESSIBILITY:
- All office facilities are wheelchair accessible
- Large-print and digital formats available for all communications
- Assistive technology (screen readers, keyboard navigation) available on request from IT
- Sign language interpreter available for formal meetings on request (2-week notice to HR)

ANTI-HARASSMENT IN INCLUSION CONTEXT:
- Any behaviour that makes a colleague feel excluded, marginalised, or unwelcome: Addressed as harassment
- Microaggressions: Small, everyday behaviours that communicate negative messages — treated seriously
- Banter that crosses the line: Report to manager or HR; intent does not excuse impact
- Investigation within 10 business days; confidentiality maintained

MEASURING INCLUSION:
- Annual Employee Engagement Survey includes DEI-specific questions
- Results shared with all employees; action plan published within 30 days
- Inclusion score tracked quarterly by HR and reported to leadership team
