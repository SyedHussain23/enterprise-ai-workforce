Company Policy Document

Topic: Internal Communication Standards & Channels

Details:
Official communication tools, standards, and best practices for all employees.

OFFICIAL COMMUNICATION CHANNELS:

Microsoft Teams (Primary Internal Tool):
- All internal messaging, quick questions, team collaboration
- Do NOT use personal WhatsApp for official work communication
- Company WhatsApp Business is approved for client-facing communication only
- Status: Set your availability (Available, Busy, Away, Do Not Disturb)
- Response time expectation: Within 2 hours during working hours
- Channel structure: #general, #it-support, #finance-help, #hr-updates, #announcements

Microsoft Outlook (Email):
- Use for formal communications, external parties, and documented decisions
- Response time: Same business day (within 8 working hours)
- Out-of-office: Must be set when on leave; include alternate contact
- Email signatures: Use company-approved signature template (download from SharePoint → Templates)
- Do NOT use personal Gmail, Yahoo, or other personal email for work

Company Announcements:
- Important company news: Teams → #announcements channel (all-hands read)
- HR updates (leave balances, policy changes): HR Portal + Teams notification
- IT system alerts: Teams → #it-alerts
- Finance deadlines: Teams → #finance-updates

COMMUNICATION ETIQUETTE:
Professional Tone:
- Use respectful, professional language in all written communications
- Avoid ALL CAPS (implies shouting); avoid aggressive or passive-aggressive tone
- Proofread before sending — spelling and grammar reflect your professionalism
- In Arabic communications: Maintain formal Arabic (Modern Standard Arabic) in written documents

Response Time Standards:
- Teams chat: Within 2 hours (core hours); within 24 hours if marked "low priority"
- Email: Same business day
- Calls: Return missed calls within 4 hours
- Urgent matters: Call directly — do not rely on email/chat for time-sensitive issues
- If you are unavailable: Set status to "Do Not Disturb" and set an auto-reply

Escalation:
- Cannot reach someone for 2+ hours on an urgent matter: Escalate to their manager
- System outage: Contact IT via email (it-support@company.com) and call Ext. 1001
- Emergency: Call the office emergency line: +971-4-XXX-0000

GROUP CHAT BEST PRACTICES:
- Only add people who need to be in the conversation
- Name group chats descriptively (e.g., "Q1 Finance Report Team" not "Chat 1")
- Pin important messages; use @mention to direct messages to specific people
- Archive (not delete) group chats when a project ends

EMAIL MANAGEMENT:
- Inbox Zero: Process emails daily; use folders to organise
- Reply-All: Use sparingly — if only the sender needs your reply, reply only to them
- CC vs BCC: CC for transparency; BCC for large mailing lists only
- File size limit: 25MB per email; use OneDrive link for larger files
- Confidential emails: Use "Sensitive" sensitivity label in Outlook; do not forward without approval

CLIENT COMMUNICATION:
- All client-facing communications must maintain high professionalism
- No informal language, slang, or abbreviations with clients
- Never share internal matters, disputes, or financials with clients
- WhatsApp Business approved for brief client messages; formal follow-up via email

SOCIAL MEDIA:
- See Code of Conduct (Company_4) for social media rules
- Official social media managed by Marketing only

CONTACTS:
- IT (Teams/Outlook issues): it-support@company.com | Ext. 1001
- HR (internal communication): hr@company.com
- Marketing (announcements): marketing@company.com
