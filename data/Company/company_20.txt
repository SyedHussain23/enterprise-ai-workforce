Company Policy Document

Topic: Feedback Culture, Employee Engagement & Surveys

Details:
How we collect, use, and act on employee feedback across all levels of the organisation.

FEEDBACK PHILOSOPHY:
Feedback is a gift. We operate a continuous, real-time feedback culture — not just once a year at appraisal time. Honest, specific, timely feedback (both positive and developmental) makes individuals, teams, and the company better.

FEEDBACK CHANNELS:

1. ONE-ON-ONE MEETINGS:
- Frequency: Minimum bi-weekly between employee and direct manager
- Focus: Work progress, blockers, development, wellbeing
- Not a status update meeting — it's a developmental conversation
- Employee should come with their own agenda items, not just wait to be asked
- Notes kept privately by manager; key action items captured

2. PERFORMANCE REVIEWS (Formal):
- Mid-year review: July — developmental focus; no formal rating
- Year-end review: December — formal rating; links to salary/bonus
- See full details in Performance Policy (Company_9)

3. 360-DEGREE FEEDBACK:
- Available to employees at Grade 5 and above (on request or manager recommendation)
- Feedback collected anonymously from: Manager, peers, direct reports, cross-functional partners
- Managed by HR using digital 360 tool
- Results shared in dedicated feedback session with HR Business Partner
- Not used directly in formal rating — developmental purpose only

4. EMPLOYEE ENGAGEMENT SURVEY (Annual):
- Annual survey: Sent to all employees in October/November
- Anonymous; 15–20 questions; takes ~10 minutes
- Topics: Management quality, career growth, DEI, wellbeing, compensation, culture
- Response rate target: 85%+ (below 75%: Leadership required to investigate)
- Results shared with all employees within 30 days of survey close
- Action plans published and tracked quarterly by HR and department heads

5. PULSE SURVEYS (Quarterly):
- Short survey: 5–7 questions
- Sent quarterly to all employees via HR Portal or email
- Results reviewed by HR within 2 weeks; trends shared with leadership
- Key focus: Identifying teams that need support early

6. EXIT INTERVIEWS:
- Conducted by HR with all departing employees (voluntary)
- Anonymous option available
- Data aggregated to identify patterns; shared with leadership annually
- Themes used to improve retention and culture

7. STAY INTERVIEWS:
- Conducted annually with high performers and key talent
- Proactive conversation: "What keeps you here? What could we do better?"
- Output: Personalised development or retention action from manager/HR

8. SUGGESTION BOX:
- Digital: HR Portal → Feedback → Submit Suggestion (anonymous option)
- Physical: Suggestion boxes in kitchen areas on all floors
- All suggestions reviewed monthly by Culture Committee
- Implemented suggestions: Publicly credited to the suggester (if they want)

ACTING ON FEEDBACK:
- Every piece of feedback received generates a response or action within 30 days
- Annual survey results: Action plan published, progress reviewed quarterly
- Individual feedback: Manager responds/acts within 5 business days
- "You Said, We Did" bulletin: Monthly internal communication showing feedback and actions taken

CONTACTS:
- Employee engagement: hr@company.com
- Survey tool access: HR Portal → Surveys
- Culture Committee (suggestions): culture@company.com
