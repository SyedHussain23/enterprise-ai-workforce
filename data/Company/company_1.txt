Company Policy Document

Topic: Working Hours, Attendance & Overtime Policy

Details:
Official working hours and attendance standards for all employees across the company.

STANDARD WORKING HOURS:
- Sunday to Thursday: 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM (UAE work week)
- Friday and Saturday: Weekend (public holiday in UAE)
- Total hours per week: 45 hours (UAE Labour Law Federal Decree-Law No. 33 of 2021)
- Lunch break: 1 hour (12:30 PM – 1:30 PM, not counted in working hours)

RAMADAN WORKING HOURS:
- All employees (regardless of religion): 6 hours/day (reduced by law — Article 17)
- Typical Ramadan schedule: 9:00 AM – 3:00 PM
- No overtime during Ramadan without specific written approval
- Ramadan dates vary each year — HR will communicate specific dates in advance

FLEXIBLE WORKING (FWA):
- Core hours: 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM (must be present/reachable)
- Flexible band: 8:00 AM – 10:00 AM (arrival) / 4:00 PM – 7:00 PM (departure)
- FWA must be approved by line manager in writing
- Not available during probation period or if performance is under review

ATTENDANCE TRACKING:
- System: Biometric/access card + HR Portal time tracking
- Grace period: 10 minutes (after which late arrival is recorded)
- Frequent late arrivals (3+ in a month): formal counselling triggered
- AWOL (absent without leave): Warning letter issued; repeated AWOL may lead to termination

OVERTIME POLICY:
- Overtime rate: 125% of hourly wage for hours beyond 8/day or 48/week
- Overtime on rest day: 150% (Friday/Saturday)
- Overtime on public holidays: 150% + compensatory day off
- Overtime must be pre-approved by line manager and submitted via HR Portal → Overtime Request
- Maximum overtime: 2 hours/day (Article 19 UAE Labour Law)
- Finance employees: Overtime processed in the following month's payroll

WORKING FROM HOME:
- Eligible employees: 2 days per week (see Remote Work Policy — Company_3)
- Not applicable during first 90 days of employment
- WFH days: Must be agreed with manager at least 24 hours in advance
- Core hours apply equally during WFH days

NIGHT SHIFT / SHIFT WORK:
- Shift employees: Receive 20–30% shift allowance on basic salary
- Night shift: 9:00 PM – 6:00 AM (attracts 25% additional pay — Article 16)
- Shift schedules communicated 2 weeks in advance

TIME-OFF IN LIEU (TOIL):
- Where overtime cannot be paid, TOIL may be granted hour-for-hour
- TOIL must be used within 90 days or it lapses
- Request via HR Portal → Leave → TOIL

CONTACTS:
- HR Portal (time management): https://hr.company.internal
- Attendance queries: attendance@company.com
- HR Helpdesk: +971-4-XXX-1000 (Sunday–Thursday, 8 AM–5 PM)

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS:
Q: Do I need to clock in if I'm working from home?
A: Yes — use HR Portal to log your WFH attendance daily.

Q: What if I'm sick and can't come in?
A: Notify your manager and HR before 9:30 AM. Log sick leave on HR Portal same day.

Q: Can I leave early and make up the time later?
A: Yes, with prior manager approval. Log as flex-time on HR Portal.

Q: Is Friday a working day?
A: No — Friday and Saturday are the standard weekend in UAE.
