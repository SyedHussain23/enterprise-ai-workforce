Company Policy Document

Topic: Company Events, Social Activities & Culture Calendar

Details:
Annual events, celebrations, and social activities organised by the company and Culture Committee.

ANNUAL COMPANY EVENTS CALENDAR:

Q1 (January–March):
- New Year Kick-Off: Annual company strategy presentation by CEO; goals shared with all staff
- Town Hall Q1: In-person + virtual; Q4 results and Q1 targets reviewed
- Valentine's Day: Culture Committee organises optional team lunch or activity
- Learning Week: Annual dedicated week for training, workshops, and guest speakers

Q2 (April–June):
- Quarterly Town Hall: Business performance update
- Eid Al Fitr Celebration: Company Eid gathering; Eid gifts distributed (AED 500 voucher equivalent)
- Annual Sports Day: Half-day event; football, cricket, padel, relay races; optional attendance
- Team Budget Day: Departments given AED 500–2,000 team outing budget to use by Q2

Q3 (July–September):
- Quarterly Town Hall: H1 results, H2 goals
- Summer Internship Programme: Final presentations and intern awards ceremony
- Employee Appreciation Week: Special breakfasts, recognition shoutouts, leadership thank-you notes

Q4 (October–December):
- UAE National Day Celebration (2 December): Office decorated in UAE colours; special breakfast; national dress welcomed
- Annual Awards Night: Company's biggest event of the year (gala dinner or offsite); awards for:
  - Employee of the Year
  - Team of the Year
  - Innovation Award
  - Customer Champion Award
  - Rising Star Award (new joiner within 2 years)
- Year-End Town Hall: Full-year results, leadership reflection
- Year-End Party: Celebration dinner or activity for all staff

RAMADAN PROGRAMME:
- Iftar Dinner: Company-organised Iftar gathering (one per year during Ramadan)
- Suhoor Outing: Optional; organised by Culture Committee
- Charity Drive: Annual Ramadan charity initiative (food packages, donations to approved organisations)
- Working hours reduced for all employees during Ramadan (6 hours/day — see Company_1)

EID CELEBRATIONS:
- Eid Al Fitr and Eid Al Adha: Office celebrations on working days adjacent to Eid
- Eid gift: AED 500 equivalent (voucher, gift, or bonus — announced by Finance/HR)
- Early departure on Eid eve: 2 hours early dismissal

SPORTS & WELLNESS EVENTS:
- Monthly wellness challenge: Steps challenge, hydration challenge, etc. — prizes for top participants
- Annual sports day: Half-day event (see Q2 above)
- Yoga / fitness classes: Weekly, arranged by Culture Committee (in-office or rooftop)
- Padel league: Quarterly inter-department padel tournament

HOW TO GET INVOLVED:
- Culture Committee: Open to all employees; meets monthly; drives event planning
- Event volunteers: Sign up via Teams → Culture Committee channel
- Event suggestions: Submit via Teams → Culture Committee → Suggestions

EVENT POLICIES:
- All events are inclusive — no mandatory participation in religious ceremonies
- Events are alcohol-free (in line with UAE cultural standards and Islamic values)
- Photos taken at events may be used for internal communications (employees can opt out)
- Events on working days: Approved as work time; no annual leave deducted
- Events outside working hours: Voluntary attendance; no pressure to attend

TEAM BUDGET:
- Each department receives AED 500–2,000 per year for team activities
- Examples: Team lunch, escape room, bowling, cultural visit, Iftar
- Approved by department head; expenses via Finance Portal → Team Events

CONTACTS:
- Culture Committee: culture@company.com
- Event announcements: Teams → #company-events
- HR (annual events queries): hr@company.com
