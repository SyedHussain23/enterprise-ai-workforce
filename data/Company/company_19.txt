Company Policy Document

Topic: Learning, Training & Development Policy

Details:
Comprehensive guide to all learning and development programmes available to employees.

LEARNING PHILOSOPHY:
We are a learning organisation. We invest in our people because developing capability today is our competitive advantage tomorrow. Every employee has a right to grow professionally — and a responsibility to do so.

TRAINING BUDGET (Annual):
- Grade 1–3: AED 5,000 per employee per year
- Grade 4–6: AED 10,000 per employee per year
- Grade 7–8: AED 15,000 per employee per year
- Budget approved by department head; managed via Finance Portal → Training Budget
- Unused budget does not roll over to next year

HOW TO REQUEST TRAINING:
1. Identify a course or programme aligned with your role/goals
2. Discuss with line manager and agree it supports development plan
3. Submit request: HR Portal → Learning → Training Request
4. Department head approves within 5 business days
5. HR arranges/confirms booking and handles payment
6. Complete training; submit certificate/completion to HR within 7 days of completion

MANDATORY TRAINING (All Employees):
The following must be completed annually:
- UAE Labour Law & Employee Rights: 30-minute online module (HR assigns in onboarding)
- Anti-Money Laundering (AML) & Compliance: 30-minute online (Compliance assigns)
- Cybersecurity Awareness (Phishing, Password Safety): 30-minute online (IT assigns)
- Fire Safety & Emergency Procedures: 30-minute online + annual drill (Facilities assigns)
- DEI Awareness: 30-minute online (HR assigns)
- Code of Conduct: Annual refresher (30-minute online)

LEARNING PLATFORMS & RESOURCES (Free for All Employees):
- LinkedIn Learning: Unlimited access — login with company email at linkedin.com/learning
- Microsoft Learn: Free Microsoft certifications and learning paths (IT-focused)
- Internal Knowledge Base: SharePoint → Learning → Company Knowledge Base
- Lunch & Learn Sessions: Monthly; recorded and posted on SharePoint
- Guest Speaker Series: Quarterly external expert sessions

PROFESSIONAL CERTIFICATIONS:
Fully or Partially Funded:
- Project Management: PMP, PRINCE2, Agile/Scrum
- Finance: ACCA, CFA, CMA, CIMA
- IT: Microsoft Azure, AWS, CompTIA, CISSP, CEH
- HR: CIPD, SHRM, HRCI
- Data & Analytics: Google Analytics, Power BI, Tableau
- Legal/Compliance: CFE, CAMS (AML certification)

Training Bond:
- Courses over AED 5,000: Training bond of 12–24 months applies
- If employee resigns within bond period: Proportional cost recovery applies
- Bond agreement signed before training commences

INTERNAL TRAINING PROGRAMMES:
- Management Development Programme (MDP): 6 months; for high-potential Grade 5+ employees
- Graduate Development Programme (GDP): 2-year rotational for fresh graduates
- "Lunch & Learn" — Internal knowledge sharing: Every first Sunday, 12:30–1:30 PM
- Shadowing Programme: Employees can shadow colleagues in other departments (1-day to 1-week)
- Mentoring Programme: 6-month structured mentoring with senior leaders

LANGUAGE TRAINING:
- Arabic language training: Available and subsidised (AED 3,000/year for non-Arabic speakers)
- Business English: Available for employees who need to strengthen professional communication
- Both approved from Learning Budget

TRACKING & REPORTING:
- All training completion tracked in HR Portal → My Learning → Completed Training
- Mandatory training completion rate monitored monthly; reminders sent by HR
- Annual training completion included in performance review discussion

CONTACTS:
- Learning queries: learning@company.com
- HR Portal (training requests): https://hr.company.internal → Learning
- LinkedIn Learning access issues: it-support@company.com
