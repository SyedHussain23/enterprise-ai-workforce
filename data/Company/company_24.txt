Company Policy Document

Topic: Company Vision, Strategic Direction & Long-Term Goals

Details:
Our company's vision for the future and the strategic pillars that guide every decision we make.

OUR VISION:
"To be the most trusted and innovative enterprise solutions partner in the UAE and GCC region, empowering organisations to achieve their full potential through technology, talent, and excellence."

WHAT THIS MEANS IN PRACTICE:
- Most Trusted: We earn trust through delivery, transparency, and integrity — not just words
- Most Innovative: We bring ideas and solutions that don't exist yet; we solve problems others haven't seen
- UAE and GCC Focus: We are deeply embedded in the region — we understand local culture, law, and business dynamics
- Empowering Organisations: Our success is measured by our clients' success
- Technology, Talent, Excellence: Our three pillars — what we invest in and lead with

STRATEGIC PILLARS (2024–2027):

1. MARKET LEADERSHIP IN UAE/GCC:
- Grow market share in Dubai, Abu Dhabi, and Saudi Arabia (top 3 priority markets)
- Win government and semi-government contracts (priority sector)
- Expand presence in financial services, healthcare, and logistics sectors

2. TECHNOLOGY INNOVATION:
- AI and automation at the core of all service offerings by 2026
- Proprietary AI platform (this system) rolled out to 50+ enterprise clients by 2026
- R&D investment: 15% of annual revenue allocated to technology development
- Patent filing target: 3 patents per year by 2025

3. PEOPLE EXCELLENCE:
- Be an employer of choice in UAE (target: Top 10 "Great Place to Work" UAE ranking by 2026)
- Emiratization: 15% UAE National workforce by 2026 (in line with NAFIS)
- Gender balance: 40% women in management by 2027
- Annual training investment: AED 10,000 per employee average

4. CLIENT SUCCESS:
- Net Promoter Score (NPS): Target 70+ by 2026 (from current 52)
- Client retention rate: 90%+
- Zero clients lost to competitor due to service failure
- Annual client satisfaction survey: 85%+ satisfaction rating

5. SUSTAINABLE GROWTH:
- Revenue growth: 25% CAGR through 2027
- Profitability: EBITDA margin 18%+ by 2026
- Geographic expansion: Open Riyadh office by Q2 2025; Doha office by Q4 2025
- ESG: Carbon footprint reduction plan published by 2025; social impact programme launched

HOW EMPLOYEES CONNECT TO THE VISION:
- Every team's annual goals (OKRs) are directly linked to one or more strategic pillars
- Employees can see how their KPIs connect to company strategy in HR Portal → Goals → Strategic Alignment
- Town Halls: CEO updates all employees on progress against vision every quarter
- Strategy document: Published on SharePoint → Company → Strategy → 2024-2027 Plan

VISION IN DAILY WORK:
- "Does this decision move us closer to our vision?" — The test for every major decision
- Innovation suggestions welcome at any level: HR Portal → Feedback → Innovation Ideas
- Employees are ambassadors of the vision — how you show up at work and with clients matters
