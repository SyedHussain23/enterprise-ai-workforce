Company Policy Document

Topic: Employee Recognition & Rewards Programme

Details:
How the company recognises and rewards outstanding performance, behaviour, and contribution.

RECOGNITION PHILOSOPHY:
Great work deserves to be seen. We believe in recognising both results and the way they are achieved — effort, collaboration, initiative, and living our values all matter.

RECOGNITION PROGRAMMES:

1. PEER RECOGNITION (Shoutout Programme):
- Any employee can give a "Shoutout" to any colleague at any time
- How to give: HR Portal → Recognition → Give a Shoutout
- Shoutout includes: Colleague's name, what they did, which company value it demonstrates
- Visible to the whole team/company (opt-in to public display)
- No approval required; immediate and easy
- Monthly "Most Recognised" employee featured in company newsletter

2. EMPLOYEE OF THE MONTH:
- Nominated by peers or managers each month
- Criteria: Outstanding performance, above-and-beyond contribution, or living company values
- Nomination deadline: Last Thursday of each month (via HR Portal → Recognition → Nominate)
- Reward: Certificate of achievement + AED 500 gift voucher + feature in company newsletter
- Winner announced at Monthly All-Hands or Team Meeting

3. EMPLOYEE OF THE QUARTER:
- Shortlisted from monthly winners and exceptional nominations
- Selected by HR + leadership panel
- Reward: AED 1,500 gift voucher + trophy + LinkedIn recommendation from CEO
- Announced at Quarterly Town Hall

4. ANNUAL AWARDS (Year-End):
- Employee of the Year: AED 5,000 cash bonus + award trophy + special mention at annual gala
- Team of the Year: AED 2,000 team experience budget + team trophy
- Innovation Award: For the best new idea implemented during the year — AED 3,000
- Customer Champion Award: For outstanding client feedback/impact — AED 3,000
- Rising Star Award: Best new joiner (under 2 years) — AED 2,000
- All awards presented at the Annual Awards Night (Q4 event)

5. SPOT BONUS PROGRAMME:
- Managers can award spot bonuses for exceptional contributions at any time
- Amount: AED 200–1,000 (approved by department head)
- Process: Manager submits via HR Portal → Recognition → Spot Bonus → Finance processes in next payroll
- No limit on frequency — but should be meaningful and specific

6. LONG-SERVICE AWARDS:
- 3 years: Certificate + AED 500 gift voucher
- 5 years: Certificate + AED 1,500 + extra day of annual leave
- 10 years: Personalised award + AED 5,000 + 3 extra days annual leave
- 15 years: Personalised award + AED 10,000 + 5 extra days annual leave
- Presented at Town Hall or team meeting (employee's preference)

7. REFERRAL BONUS:
- Employees who refer a successful candidate receive AED 2,000–5,000 (based on role seniority)
- Paid after referred employee completes 3-month probation
- Process: HR Portal → Recruitment → Refer a Colleague

PERFORMANCE-LINKED REWARDS:
- Annual performance bonus: 5–40% of basic salary (grade-dependent); based on rating (see Company_9)
- Salary increases: Tied to performance rating; effective April each year
- Fast-track promotion: Outstanding performers eligible for off-cycle promotion

HOW RECOGNITION LINKS TO PERFORMANCE REVIEW:
- Shoutouts and recognition received are visible to managers during appraisal
- Being a recognised team player positively influences rating on behavioural competencies
- Nominations for awards noted in employee file

CONTACTS:
- Recognition programme: HR Portal → Recognition
- Spot bonus requests: Manager submits via HR Portal
- Annual awards: culture@company.com
