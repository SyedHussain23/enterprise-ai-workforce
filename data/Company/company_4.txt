Company Policy Document

Topic: Code of Conduct & Professional Behaviour

Details:
Standards of professional conduct expected from all employees, contractors, and representatives.

CORE PRINCIPLES:
1. Integrity — Act honestly in all interactions, internal and external
2. Respect — Treat every colleague, client, and partner with dignity
3. Accountability — Take ownership of your actions and decisions
4. Excellence — Deliver quality work and continuously improve
5. Collaboration — Work as one team across departments and nationalities

WHAT IS EXPECTED:
Professional Conduct:
- Represent the company positively at all times, including on social media
- Maintain confidentiality of company information, client data, and business strategies
- Avoid and disclose any conflicts of interest to your manager and HR immediately
- Attend scheduled meetings prepared and on time
- Meet deadlines and communicate proactively if at risk
- Treat company assets (laptop, phone, vehicle) with care; minimal personal use only

Respect & Inclusion:
- Zero tolerance for bullying, harassment, discrimination, or intimidation
- Respect UAE's cultural values, traditions, and Islamic customs
- Avoid divisive discussions about religion, politics, or ethnicity in the workplace
- Use inclusive language — avoid slang or idioms that exclude others

CONFLICTS OF INTEREST:
- Disclose any outside employment, business interests, or personal relationships with competitors/vendors
- Family members employed at the same vendor or client: Must be disclosed immediately
- Submit via: HR Portal → Ethics → Conflict of Interest Declaration
- Undisclosed conflicts: Grounds for disciplinary action up to termination

GIFTS, ENTERTAINMENT & HOSPITALITY:
- Gifts from vendors/clients: Acceptable up to AED 200 in value
- Gifts exceeding AED 200: Must be declared to Finance and either returned or surrendered
- Entertainment/client meals: Limit AED 300/person; expense via Finance Portal
- Facilitation payments (paying to speed up government services): Strictly prohibited under UAE law
- Cash gifts: Never acceptable under any circumstances
- Bribes offered or received: Immediate termination + legal referral to UAE authorities

SOCIAL MEDIA CONDUCT:
- Do not post confidential company information, financials, client data, or internal matters
- Personal opinions must be clearly distinct from company views (add disclaimer)
- Do not tag the company in personal posts without Marketing team approval
- Defamatory, misleading, or damaging posts about the company or colleagues: Disciplinary action

REPORTING MISCONDUCT:
- Report to: Line manager or HR (first instance)
- Anonymous reporting: ethics@company.com | Ethics Hotline: +971-4-XXX-9999 (24/7)
- Whistleblower protection: Reporters are fully protected from retaliation under UAE law
- All reports investigated within 10 business days; reporter kept informed

DISCIPLINARY CONSEQUENCES:
- Minor violations: Verbal warning → Written warning → Final written warning
- Major violations (fraud, harassment, bribery, theft): Immediate termination under Article 44, UAE Labour Law No. 33 of 2021

CONTACTS:
- Ethics Hotline: ethics@company.com | +971-4-XXX-9999
- HR Department: hr@company.com
- Compliance Officer: compliance@company.com
