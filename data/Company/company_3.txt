Company Policy Document

Topic: Remote Work & Work From Home (WFH) Policy

Details:
Comprehensive guidelines for working remotely, applicable to all eligible employees.

ELIGIBILITY:
- All permanent employees who have completed their 90-day probation period
- Role must be compatible with remote work (as determined by line manager)
- Employee must have a stable internet connection and a dedicated, distraction-free workspace
- Employees on active Performance Improvement Plans (PIP) are not eligible

WFH ENTITLEMENT:
- Maximum: 2 days per week
- Days must be agreed with the line manager by end of Thursday for the following week
- WFH is a privilege, not a right — it can be suspended for performance or business reasons

WFH APPROVAL PROCESS:
1. Submit request via HR Portal → WFH → New Request (at least 24 hours in advance)
2. Line manager approves or declines
3. Approved WFH days are logged automatically in attendance system
4. Ad-hoc emergency WFH (e.g., child sick at home): Notify manager by 9:00 AM via Teams

MANDATORY WFH REQUIREMENTS:
Technical:
- VPN connected at all times via Cisco AnyConnect
- Work-issued device only (no personal computers for company work)
- Microsoft Teams status must be set to "Available" during core hours (10 AM–4 PM)
- Camera on during all meetings unless bandwidth issues (notify team)

Security:
- Do not work from public Wi-Fi (cafes, airports) without VPN
- Lock screen when stepping away from device
- Never leave work documents visible to family members or others
- Report any security incidents immediately to it-support@company.com

Productivity:
- Same performance standards apply as in-office
- Respond to messages within 30 minutes during core hours
- Update calendar with out-of-office or blocked time for focus work

INTERNET ALLOWANCE:
- AED 150/month internet allowance for approved WFH employees
- Submit via Finance Portal → Allowances → Internet Allowance (monthly by 25th)

WHAT CANNOT BE DONE REMOTELY:
- Tasks requiring physical access to servers or on-site equipment
- In-person client meetings (cannot be substituted with video)
- Handling confidential physical documents
- Jobs designated as "office-only" by department head

HYBRID SCHEDULE EXAMPLE:
- Office days (3/week): Meetings, collaboration, client visits
- WFH days (2/week): Deep work, report writing, analysis, online training

MONITORING:
- IT monitors VPN connection status, not personal activity
- HR reviews WFH patterns quarterly — consistent underperformance on WFH days may lead to restriction
- Work output is measured by deliverables, not hours logged

INTERNATIONAL / OVERSEAS WORKING:
- Working from outside UAE requires prior written approval from CHRO
- Tax and visa implications must be assessed before approval
- Approved on case-by-case basis only (maximum 30 days per year)

EQUIPMENT SUPPORT:
- If equipment fails during WFH: Contact IT via Teams or ext. 1001 immediately
- Loan equipment is available for emergencies — collect from IT on next office day

CONTACTS:
- WFH requests: HR Portal → WFH
- Technical support: it-support@company.com | Ext. 1001
- Policy queries: hr@company.com

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS:
Q: Can I work from a coffee shop or coworking space?
A: Yes, if you use VPN and ensure no one can see your screen. Never use public Wi-Fi without VPN.

Q: What if my internet goes down during WFH?
A: Notify your manager immediately via mobile. You may need to come into the office.

Q: Does WFH apply during Ramadan?
A: Yes — WFH entitlement stays the same during Ramadan.
