Finance Policy Document

Topic: Cost Control and Financial Efficiency Policy

Details:
All employees play a role in managing company costs responsibly.

COST CONSCIOUSNESS CULTURE:
- Treat company money as you would your own — spend where it adds value, save where it doesn't
- Every AED saved on unnecessary costs directly improves company profitability and ability to invest in employees and growth
- Cost savings are recognized and rewarded in performance reviews

COST CONTROL PRINCIPLES:
1. Budget discipline: Do not spend beyond approved budget without Finance approval
2. Value for money: Always compare options before committing to a purchase
3. Competitive procurement: Get multiple quotes for significant purchases (see Procurement Policy)
4. Waste reduction: Avoid waste — unused licenses, idle assets, unnecessary prints, energy waste

COMMON COST REDUCTION ACTIONS (Employees Can Take):
- Print only when necessary — use digital documents
- Use video conferencing instead of travel where possible
- Return unused office supplies rather than hoarding
- Cancel unused software subscriptions (report to IT to release licenses)
- Use economy class for short-haul travel
- Choose mid-range hotels within policy limits
- Book travel well in advance (saves 20–40% on flights)
- Share taxis and Uber rides to same destination with colleagues

REPORTING COST WASTE:
- See obvious waste or inefficiency? Report to your manager or Finance
- Finance Portal → Ideas → Cost Saving Idea submission
- Verified savings above AED 5,000/year: Recognized in quarterly awards
- Employee suggestion implemented saving >AED 50,000/year: AED 1,000–5,000 cash reward

DEPARTMENTAL COST REPORTING:
- Department heads receive monthly cost report vs budget
- 10%+ overspend must be explained in writing to Finance Director
- Three consecutive months overspending: Finance review meeting with senior management

COST SAVING INITIATIVES:
- Company conducts annual cost efficiency review (July–August)
- Departments asked to identify 5–10% cost reduction opportunities
- Savings identified do not necessarily reduce department budget permanently — reinvested in priorities
- Travel cost reduction: Finance tracks travel spend; quarterly travel report to CFO

ENERGY EFFICIENCY (UAE Sustainability):
- UAE Net Zero 2050 commitment — company contributes to national sustainability goals
- Turn off lights, AC, and equipment when leaving meeting rooms and offices
- Report facilities waste (running water, lights left on overnight) to Facilities team
- Company targets annual 10% reduction in energy consumption
