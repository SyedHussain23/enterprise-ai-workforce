Finance Policy Document

Topic: Finance Department Services and Self-Service Guide

Details:
Complete guide to Finance services available to employees and how to access them.

FINANCE PORTAL:
URL: https://finance.company.internal
Login: Company email and password (SSO)
Available 24/7 for self-service tasks

FINANCE SELF-SERVICE SERVICES:

Payroll and Salary:
- Download payslips (HR Portal → Payroll → My Payslips)
- Submit salary queries (by 20th of month for same-month resolution)
- Request salary advance (Finance Portal → Salary Advance)
- Request salary certificate for bank or visa (HR Portal → My Documents)

Expenses:
- Submit expense claims (Finance Portal → Expense Claims)
- Track claim status (Finance Portal → Expense Claims → My Claims)
- Submit allowance requests (Finance Portal → Allowances)
- Travel expense reconciliation (Finance Portal → Travel → Reconcile)

Procurement:
- Raise purchase orders (Finance Portal → Procurement → New PO)
- Track PO status (Finance Portal → Procurement → My POs)
- Register new vendor (Finance Portal → Vendors → New Registration)

Corporate Cards:
- View monthly statement (Finance Portal → Corporate Cards → Statement)
- Submit monthly reconciliation (Finance Portal → Corporate Cards → Reconcile)
- Report lost card (call bank immediately, then Finance Portal → Cards → Report Lost)

COMMON FINANCE REQUESTS AND TURNAROUND:
- Expense claim reimbursement: 5–7 business days from approval
- Salary advance: 3–5 business days from approval
- PO issuance: 2–3 business days from approval
- Vendor payment: Within agreed payment terms (Net 30 standard)
- Salary certificate: 3 business days (HR Portal)
- Budget vs actuals report: By 5th of each month (Finance sends automatically)

FREQUENTLY ASKED FINANCE QUESTIONS:
Q: When will my expense claim be reimbursed? A: Next payroll cycle after Finance approval (or mid-month for urgent >AED 1,000 claims)
Q: How do I know my salary advance was approved? A: Email notification from Finance Portal + funds in account within 3 business days
Q: My payslip shows wrong salary — what do I do? A: Email payroll@company.com before 20th of month for same-month correction
Q: How do I add a new supplier? A: Finance Portal → Vendors → Register New Vendor (allow 5–7 days)
Q: Can I pay for something and claim back? A: Yes, via Expense Claims — but only for eligible expenses under AED 2,000 without prior PO
Q: How do I calculate my gratuity? A: Finance Portal → Tools → Gratuity Calculator
