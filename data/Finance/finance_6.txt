Finance Policy Document

Topic: Budget Management and Planning

Details:
The annual budget process ensures resources are allocated efficiently to meet business objectives.

BUDGET CYCLE:
- Budget preparation: October–November (for following year)
- Submission deadline: 30th November
- CFO review and consolidation: December
- Board approval: January (before fiscal year start)
- Budget communicated to department heads: By 15th January

BUDGET COMPONENTS:
1. Operating Expenses (OPEX): Day-to-day costs — salaries, rent, utilities, software, marketing
2. Capital Expenditure (CAPEX): Long-term assets — equipment, software licenses > 1 year, renovations
3. Headcount Plan: Number of employees, hiring plan, timing
4. Revenue Forecast: Expected income (prepared by Sales/Finance together)

DEPARTMENT BUDGET PREPARATION:
1. Finance sends each department head the prior year actuals and next year templates
2. Department head completes: Staff costs, operational costs, project costs, travel budget
3. Justification required for any item > 20% increase vs prior year
4. Submit via Finance Portal → Budget → Department Submission
5. Finance partner reviews with department head in calibration meeting (November)

MONTHLY BUDGET MONITORING:
- Finance sends monthly budget vs actuals report to each department head by 5th of following month
- Report includes: Budget, Actual, Variance (AED and %), YTD summary
- Department heads must investigate variances > 10% and report to Finance
- Underspend: Generally acceptable, unspent budget doesn't carry forward to next year
- Overspend: Requires Finance Director approval and action plan to correct

MID-YEAR BUDGET REVISION:
- Formal mid-year review in July
- Departments may request budget adjustments (increase or reallocation)
- Net budget increase requests require CFO approval
- Budget reallocation between cost lines within same department: Department head can approve

REQUESTING UNBUDGETED SPEND:
1. Submit unbudgeted spend request to Finance Portal → Budget → Unbudgeted Request
2. Include: Amount, description, business justification, which budget line to fund from
3. Approval chain: Department Head → Finance Director → CFO (if > AED 50,000)
4. Decision within 5 business days

COST CENTER CODES:
- Each department has a cost center code
- All expenses must be coded to correct cost center
- Finance provides cost center guide (Finance Portal → Resources → Cost Center List)
- Wrong cost center: Finance corrects after notifying department head
