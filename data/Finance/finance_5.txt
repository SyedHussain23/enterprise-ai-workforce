Finance Policy Document

Topic: Invoice Processing and Vendor Payments

Details:
The company processes supplier invoices and vendor payments in accordance with agreed payment terms.

INVOICE SUBMISSION (For Suppliers/Vendors):
- Submit invoices to: accounts.payable@company.com
- Format: PDF (scanned clear originals)
- Required information on invoice:
  * Supplier name and address
  * Supplier TRN (if VAT registered)
  * Company name (as named in PO)
  * Purchase Order (PO) number — mandatory
  * Invoice number and date
  * Description of goods/services
  * Net amount, VAT (5%), total amount
  * Bank details for payment (IBAN, SWIFT/BIC)
  * Payment terms agreed

STANDARD PAYMENT TERMS:
- Default: Net 30 days from invoice date
- Negotiated terms: As per signed contract/PO
- Early payment discount: If offered by supplier, Finance evaluates case-by-case
- Government/utility suppliers: Per their standard terms

PAYMENT PROCESS:
1. Invoice received by Finance team
2. Matched against Purchase Order (3-way match: PO, goods receipt, invoice)
3. Department head confirms goods/services received (via Finance Portal notification)
4. Finance approves for payment
5. Payment processed via bank transfer (IBAN)
6. Remittance advice sent to supplier on payment date

PAYMENT APPROVAL THRESHOLDS:
- Up to AED 10,000: Finance Manager approval
- AED 10,000–50,000: Finance Director approval
- Above AED 50,000: CFO or CEO approval
- Capital expenditure (CAPEX) over AED 100,000: Board approval required

PURCHASE ORDER (PO) REQUIREMENT:
- All purchases above AED 500 require a PO
- PO must be raised BEFORE goods/services received (retroactive POs require Finance Director approval)
- Raise PO via Finance Portal → Procurement → Create PO
- PO approved by department head and Finance before issuing to supplier

LATE PAYMENTS:
- Company targets paying all invoices within agreed terms
- Disputed invoices: Finance contacts supplier within 5 days of receipt
- Late payment interest: As per contract terms (company policy: not to incur late payment charges)
- Supplier payment queries: accounts.payable@company.com

EMPLOYEE-SUBMITTED INVOICES (Freelancers/Consultants):
- Must have valid UAE trade license (or be company-registered entity)
- Invoice requirements same as above
- Payment terms: Net 15 days for individual consultants
- Require signed service agreement before engagement
