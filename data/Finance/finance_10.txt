Finance Policy Document

Topic: Employee Allowances and Benefits Payments

Details:
The company provides various financial allowances to support employees' work-related costs.

STANDARD MONTHLY ALLOWANCES (Included in salary):

Housing Allowance:
- Purpose: Contribution toward UAE accommodation costs (UAE has no rent control for most properties)
- Amount: AED 3,000–12,000/month (varies by grade, marital status, and number of dependents)
- Single: Lower band | Married: Higher band | With children: Additional supplement
- Paid as part of monthly salary — employee arranges own accommodation

Transport Allowance:
- Purpose: Contribution toward commuting and transportation costs
- Amount: AED 1,000–3,500/month (grade-dependent)
- Grade 1–3: AED 1,000–1,500/month
- Grade 4–5: AED 2,000–2,500/month
- Grade 6+: Company car policy applies instead of transport allowance

Communication Allowance:
- Purpose: Business phone calls on personal number
- Amount: AED 150–300/month reimbursement or prepaid company SIM
- Grade 4+ eligible for company SIM
- Claim: Finance Portal → Allowances → Phone Reimbursement (attach itemized bill)

ADDITIONAL ONE-TIME OR ANNUAL ALLOWANCES:

Annual Flight Ticket:
- One economy round trip to home country per year
- Grade 5+: Business class for flights over 6 hours
- Must book through company travel portal
- OR cash equivalent (capped at AED 3,000 per person economy, AED 6,000 business class)
- Unused flight ticket does not carry forward to next year

Furniture Allowance (On Joining):
- New joiners receive one-time furniture allowance: AED 5,000–15,000 (grade-dependent)
- For setting up new home in UAE
- Claim within first 90 days of joining
- Finance Portal → Allowances → Joining Allowance

Education Allowance (Dependent Children):
- Available from Grade 5+
- Up to AED 25,000 per child per year (maximum 3 children)
- Submit school fee invoice to Finance by September each year
- Finance Portal → Allowances → Education Claim

ALLOWANCE CLAIMS PROCESS:
1. Finance Portal → Allowances → Select Allowance Type
2. Upload supporting documentation (receipts, invoices, school fee letter)
3. Manager approval (automated request sent)
4. Finance reviews and pays in next payroll cycle
5. All allowances subject to company policy limits

ALLOWANCE TAXATION:
- UAE: Allowances are not taxable (no income tax)
- Expats with foreign tax obligations: Consult personal tax advisor for home country treatment
