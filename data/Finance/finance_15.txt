Topic: UAE End of Service Gratuity — Finance Calculation Guide

Details:
End of Service Gratuity (EOSB) is a statutory obligation under UAE Labour Law, managed and funded by Finance.

LEGAL BASIS:
Federal Decree-Law No. 33 of 2021 on the Regulation of Labour Relations in UAE

WHO PAYS GRATUITY:
- Employer pays gratuity from company funds (no employee contribution)
- Finance provisions gratuity liability monthly in financial accounts (accrual basis)
- Gratuity fund maintained as part of company financial reserves

CALCULATION METHOD:
Step 1: Determine basic salary
(Gratuity is based on BASIC SALARY ONLY — not housing, transport, or other allowances)

Step 2: Calculate daily basic rate
Daily rate = Monthly basic salary ÷ 30

Step 3: Apply entitlement per years of service:
Years 1–5: 21 calendar days per year of service
After Year 5: 30 calendar days per year (incremental)

Step 4: Calculate total gratuity
Example: Employee with 7 years service, AED 15,000 basic salary:
- Daily rate = AED 15,000 / 30 = AED 500/day
- First 5 years: 5 × 21 × AED 500 = AED 52,500
- Years 6–7: 2 × 30 × AED 500 = AED 30,000
- Total gratuity = AED 82,500

MAXIMUM GRATUITY CAP:
- UAE law caps gratuity at 2 years' total basic salary
- AED 15,000/month × 24 = AED 360,000 maximum cap
- In above example: AED 82,500 (below cap, full amount payable)

RESIGNATION ENTITLEMENT (Proportional):
- Less than 1 year: No gratuity
- 1–3 years: One-third of calculated gratuity
- 3–5 years: Two-thirds of calculated gratuity
- 5+ years: Full calculated gratuity

TERMINATION BY EMPLOYER:
- Employee receives full calculated gratuity regardless of service duration (if over 1 year)
- Unless terminated for gross misconduct under Article 44 (theft, serious offence)

GRATUITY AND DEWS:
- Some free zones (DIFC, ADGM) use DEWS (Workplace Savings Scheme) instead of gratuity
- DEWS: Monthly employer contribution of 5.83% of basic salary to portable fund
- Employee owns DEWS account and takes it on leaving — more portable than gratuity
- Finance manages DEWS contributions for eligible employees

GRATUITY CALCULATION TOOL:
- HR Portal → Payroll → Gratuity Calculator
- Inputs: Start date, current basic salary, reason for leaving
- Provides instant estimate (Finance confirms exact amount in final settlement)
