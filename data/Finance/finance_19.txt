Finance Policy Document

Topic: Contract Management and Legal Agreements

Details:
All contracts and legal agreements must go through the proper review and approval process before signing.

CONTRACT TYPES:
1. Client contracts: Service agreements, Master Service Agreements (MSAs), Statements of Work (SOWs)
2. Supplier contracts: Vendor agreements, purchase contracts, SLAs
3. Employee agreements: Employment contracts, NDAs, non-competes (HR manages)
4. Partnership agreements: JVs, revenue sharing, referral agreements
5. Lease agreements: Office, equipment, vehicle leases

CONTRACT APPROVAL PROCESS:
1. Business owner prepares contract draft or receives supplier draft
2. Legal team reviews (mandatory for any contract with liability > AED 50,000)
3. Finance reviews commercial terms (payment schedule, penalties, liabilities)
4. Signing authority:
   - Under AED 50,000 annual value: Department Director
   - AED 50,000–500,000: CFO
   - Over AED 500,000: CEO (and Board for strategic agreements)
5. Signed contract filed in Contract Management System (CMS)

NO VERBAL CONTRACTS:
- Never agree to services or purchases verbally without written agreement
- Email confirmation is the minimum — proper contract required for any significant commitment
- Verbal commitments to clients (especially pricing or delivery) must be confirmed in writing within 24 hours

CONTRACT REGISTER:
- All signed contracts stored in Finance Portal → Contracts → Contract Register
- Contracts indexed by: Type, counterparty, start date, end date, value
- Renewal reminders: Automatic alerts 90 days before expiry
- Expired contracts: Finance and legal team review for renewal or termination

KEY CONTRACT TERMS TO FLAG TO FINANCE:
- Payment terms (invoice schedule, milestone payments)
- Penalty clauses (late delivery, non-performance penalties)
- Indemnification clauses (company liability for client losses)
- Termination clauses (notice period, cost of early termination)
- Price escalation clauses (automatic annual increases)

NDA (NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT):
- Required before sharing any confidential company or client information with third parties
- Standard company NDA available: Legal Portal → Templates → NDA
- Must be signed before any sales demo, vendor evaluation, or partnership discussion
- Do not share financial information, client data, or technical details without signed NDA

CONTRACT DISPUTES:
- Client disputes: Escalate immediately to your Department Head and Finance
- Do not make commitments (credits, payments, refunds) to resolve disputes without Finance Director approval
- Legal disputes: Refer to company legal counsel immediately
