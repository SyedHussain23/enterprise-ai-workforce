Finance Policy Document

Topic: Expense Reimbursement Policy

Details:
The company reimburses legitimate business expenses incurred by employees in performing their duties.

ELIGIBLE EXPENSES:
- Business meals and client entertainment (with client present)
- Local taxi/Uber for business travel (not commuting)
- Office supplies purchased for urgent business need
- Business phone calls on personal phone (see Communication Allowance)
- Conference and training registration fees (see Training Policy)
- Business travel (see Travel and Entertainment Policy)

NON-REIMBURSABLE EXPENSES:
- Personal meals (unless on approved business travel away from base city)
- Alcohol (UAE is a Muslim country — alcohol may be consumed in licensed venues but company does not reimburse)
- Personal transportation (commuting from home to office)
- Personal items (clothing, grooming, personal care)
- Fines (parking, traffic, etc.)
- Personal entertainment
- Expenses without proper VAT invoice

EXPENSE LIMITS (Without pre-approval):
- Business meal (client): Up to AED 300 per person per meal
- Business meal (team internal): Up to AED 150 per person per meal
- Taxi/Uber (local): Up to AED 100 per trip
- Stationery/office supplies: Up to AED 200 per purchase
- Over these limits: Require manager pre-approval before spending

EXPENSE SUBMISSION PROCESS:
1. Incur expense for legitimate business purpose
2. Collect VAT invoice/receipt (not just credit card slip)
3. Submit within 30 days of incurring expense (expenses older than 30 days may be declined)
4. Go to Finance Portal → Expense Claims → New Claim
5. Fill in: Date, category, amount, purpose, client/project name
6. Upload clear photo/scan of VAT invoice
7. Submit — claim goes to manager for approval
8. Manager approved → Finance reviews → Processed in next payroll cycle

PROCESSING TIMELINE:
- Manager approval: Within 48 hours
- Finance review: Within 3 business days
- Payment: Included in monthly payroll (or separate mid-month payment for claims > AED 1,000)
- Reimbursement directly to bank account (not cash)

CORPORATE CREDIT CARD:
- Available to Grade 5+ employees and frequent business travelers
- Must reconcile card expenses monthly (Finance Portal → Corporate Cards → Monthly Reconciliation)
- Personal expenses on company card: Must be repaid immediately — disciplinary action for misuse
- Card limit: AED 5,000–25,000 based on role and usage pattern

CASH ADVANCE FOR TRAVEL:
- Request cash advance for travel via Finance Portal → Travel → Cash Advance Request
- Advance issued up to 5 days before travel
- Reconcile within 5 days of returning (submit receipts for all spending)
- Unused advance returned to Finance immediately upon return
