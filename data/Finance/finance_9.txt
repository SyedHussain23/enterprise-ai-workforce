Finance Policy Document

Topic: Payroll Processing and Salary Deductions

Details:
Detailed breakdown of how payroll is processed and what deductions may apply.

PAYROLL PROCESSING CALENDAR:
- Payroll data cutoff: 20th of each month
- Payroll processing: 21st–24th of each month
- Payslips available: 25th of each month
- Payment to bank: Last working day of month

PAYROLL TEAM CONTACTS:
- Payroll queries: payroll@company.com
- Payroll Manager: payroll.manager@company.com
- Query submission deadline: 20th of month for same-month correction
- After 20th: Correction in next month's payroll

SALARY COMPONENTS TAXED (UAE — No income tax):
- UAE has no personal income tax
- Employees receive 100% of gross salary package
- Social insurance: No mandatory social insurance for non-UAE nationals (expatriates)
- UAE Nationals: Enrolled in GPSSA (General Pension and Social Security Authority)
  * Employee contribution: 5% of basic salary
  * Employer contribution: 12.5% of basic salary
  * Total: 17.5% — deducted from Emirati employees' payroll

TYPES OF DEDUCTIONS:
1. Absence deduction: Unpaid leave days × daily rate
2. Late arrival deduction: Per attendance policy
3. Salary advance repayment: Agreed installments
4. Company loan repayment: Per signed loan agreement
5. GPSSA contributions: UAE Nationals only (5% employee share)
6. DEWS contributions: If enrolled in Dubai Employee Workplace Savings Scheme
7. Training bond recovery: If resigning during bond period

HOW DEDUCTIONS ARE CALCULATED:
- Daily rate = Monthly basic salary / 30 days (or actual calendar days if specified in contract)
- Hourly rate = Daily rate / 9 hours
- Late deduction: Only applied after 3 occurrences of lateness per month
- Absence: Full day deducted for unauthorized absence

OVERPAYMENT RECOVERY:
- If overpaid by Finance error: Employee notified in writing
- Recovery: Maximum 20% of net monthly salary per month (not one lump deduction)
- Employee must acknowledge and agree recovery plan
- Recovery in 3–6 installments for larger amounts

FINAL SETTLEMENT PAYMENT:
- Paid within 14 days of last working day (UAE Labour Law requirement)
- Includes: Final salary, leave encashment, gratuity, any outstanding bonuses
- Any deductions: Loans, company advances, damage to company property
- Final settlement statement issued in writing

PAYROLL RECORDS:
- Payslips retained for 7 years (UAE legal requirement)
- Always available via HR Portal — download copies for personal records
- Salary certificates (for visa, bank, school enrollment): Ordered via HR Portal
