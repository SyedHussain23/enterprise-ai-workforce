Finance Policy Document

Topic: Salary Advance Policy

Details:
The company provides salary advances to employees facing genuine financial need.

ELIGIBILITY:
- Minimum 6 months of service
- Satisfactory performance (no active disciplinary proceedings)
- Current salary advance fully repaid before requesting another
- Maximum 1 salary advance per 6-month period

ADVANCE AMOUNT LIMITS:
- Maximum: 50% of monthly basic salary
- Grade 1–3: Up to AED 5,000
- Grade 4–5: Up to AED 10,000
- Grade 6+: Up to AED 20,000
- Exceptional circumstances (e.g., medical emergency): Up to 100% of basic salary with HR Director approval

REPAYMENT:
- Deducted equally over next 3 months from salary
- Larger advances (>50% basic): Up to 6 months repayment schedule
- Early repayment permitted (inform Finance)
- No interest charged (interest-free advance)
- If employee leaves before full repayment: Remaining balance deducted from final settlement

HOW TO APPLY:
1. Finance Portal → Salary Advance → New Request
2. Fill in: Amount requested, reason, preferred repayment period
3. Supporting documentation required for medical/emergency advances over 50% (hospital bills, etc.)
4. Manager approval → HR review → Finance approval
5. Processing time: 3–5 business days
6. Payment: Direct transfer to salary account or in next payroll

VALID REASONS FOR ADVANCE:
- Medical emergency (for employee or immediate family)
- Essential home repairs or setup costs (new to UAE)
- Educational fees for children (term start)
- Emergency family situation requiring travel
- Advance may be declined if deemed non-essential by Finance

IMPORTANT NOTES:
- Salary advance is an advance of your own salary — not a loan
- Does not affect bonus eligibility
- Repayment plan signed and acknowledged before advance issued
- Third advance in same calendar year requires CFO approval
- Cash advances for business travel: Separate process (see Travel Policy)

ALTERNATIVE FINANCIAL SUPPORT:
- UAE banks offer personal loans to employees (company can provide salary assignment letter on request)
- Salary certificate for bank loan: HR Portal → My Documents → Salary Certificate
- Some banks offer salary advance services directly to account holders
