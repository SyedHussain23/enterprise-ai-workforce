Finance Policy Document

Topic: Bonus and Incentive Policy

Details:
The company rewards high-performing employees through a structured annual bonus program.

BONUS ELIGIBILITY:
- Must be employed on 31st December to be eligible for annual bonus
- Minimum performance rating of 3 (Meets Expectations) required
- Must have completed probation period before bonus calculation date
- Employees on Performance Improvement Plan (PIP) are not eligible
- Part-time employees: Bonus prorated based on hours worked

BONUS PAYMENT TIMING:
- Annual performance bonus: Paid in February (for prior calendar year performance)
- Mid-year incentive (certain roles): Paid in August
- Spot bonuses (exceptional contribution): Paid in next payroll cycle after approval

BONUS CALCULATION:
Bonus = Target Bonus % × Basic Annual Salary × Performance Multiplier

Target Bonus Percentages by Grade:
- Grade 1–2: 5% of annual basic salary
- Grade 3–4: 10% of annual basic salary
- Grade 5: 15% of annual basic salary
- Grade 6: 20% of annual basic salary
- Grade 7+: 25–40% of annual basic salary (includes company performance component)

Performance Multipliers:
- Rating 5 (Outstanding): 1.5× target bonus
- Rating 4 (Exceeds Expectations): 1.2× target bonus
- Rating 3 (Meets Expectations): 1.0× target bonus
- Rating 2 (Below Expectations): 0.5× target bonus
- Rating 1 (Unsatisfactory): 0× (no bonus)

Example:
Grade 3 employee, AED 10,000 basic/month, Rating 4:
Annual basic = AED 120,000
Target bonus = 10% = AED 12,000
With multiplier (1.2): AED 14,400 bonus

MID-YEAR JOINERS:
- Prorated based on months worked in the bonus year
- Joined July 1st: Eligible for 50% of full bonus (6 out of 12 months)
- Prorated to nearest full month

SALES INCENTIVE PLANS (COMMISSION):
- Sales roles have separate commission structure defined in employment contract
- Commission paid monthly (following month after deals close)
- No cap on commission earnings for outperformance
- Clawback applies if client cancels within 90 days of deal close

SPOT BONUSES:
- Manager can nominate employee for spot bonus (AED 500 – AED 5,000)
- Department Head and HR approval required
- Max 2 spot bonuses per employee per year
- Does not affect annual bonus calculation
