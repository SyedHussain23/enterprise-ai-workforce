Finance Policy Document

Topic: Insurance and Risk Management

Details:
The company maintains comprehensive insurance coverage to protect employees, assets, and operations.

EMPLOYEE INSURANCE COVERAGE:

1. Group Medical Insurance:
   - Provider: Premium insurer (Daman/AXA/Bupa)
   - Coverage: Employee + spouse + up to 3 children (under 18)
   - Inpatient limit: AED 500,000/person/year
   - Outpatient: AED 500 per visit, extensive network
   - Emergency: Covered worldwide
   - Maternity: Covered (AED 10,000–20,000 for normal delivery; AED 15,000–30,000 for C-section)
   - Dental: Limited (AED 3,000/year for extractions, fillings — cosmetic not covered)
   - Optical: Limited (AED 1,500/year for glasses or contact lenses)

2. Group Life Insurance:
   - Death benefit: 3× annual basic salary paid to named beneficiary
   - Accidental death: Additional 2× annual basic salary
   - Total permanent disability: 2× annual basic salary
   - Coverage from Day 1, beneficiary form completed within 30 days
   - Beneficiary update: HR Portal → My Benefits → Life Insurance Beneficiary

3. Workers' Compensation / Workmen's Compensation:
   - Covers work-related injury or illness
   - UAE law mandatory coverage for all employees
   - Wage continuation during treatment period (as per UAE Labour Law)
   - Permanent disability: Compensation per UAE Labour Law schedule

COMPANY INSURANCE (Assets):

4. Property Insurance:
   - Office premises, furniture, equipment: Covered for fire, theft, accidental damage
   - Company vehicles: Comprehensive motor insurance
   - IT equipment: Covered under company all-risk policy

5. Professional Indemnity Insurance:
   - Covers company for claims arising from professional services
   - Required for client-facing professional service companies

6. Directors & Officers Insurance:
   - Covers senior management against personal liability for company decisions

MEDICAL INSURANCE PRACTICAL GUIDE:
- Insurance card: Received from HR on joining (or within 2 weeks)
- Pre-approval: Required for planned admissions, surgeries, specialist referrals
- Emergency: Go directly to any hospital — pre-approval not required for emergencies
- Out-of-network: May be covered at lower rate — check Finance Portal → Benefits → Insurance Network
- Claim reimbursement (for out-of-network): Submit claim within 60 days of treatment via Finance Portal

INSURANCE CARD LOST OR EXPIRED:
- Request replacement: HR Portal → My Benefits → Insurance → Request New Card
- Turnaround: 3–5 business days
- Temporary: HR can provide insurance confirmation letter for use until card arrives
