Finance Policy Document

Topic: Travel and Entertainment Policy

Details:
This policy governs business travel and client entertainment expenses.

TRAVEL BOOKING:
- All business travel must be booked via the company travel portal or designated travel agent
- Do NOT book travel using personal loyalty cards/points (then claim cost from company)
- Economy class: All flights under 4 hours
- Business class: Flights over 6 hours for Grade 5+ employees and all employees on medical recommendation
- Flights booked minimum 7 days in advance where possible (saves cost)
- Travel approval required from manager before booking (Finance Portal → Travel → Travel Request)

ACCOMMODATION:
- Approved hotel categories:
  * Grade 1–4: Up to 4-star or AED 600/night
  * Grade 5+: Up to 5-star or AED 1,000/night
- Must use company travel portal for hotel booking
- Personal accommodation with friends/family: Reimburse equivalent hotel rate or actual cost (whichever is lower)
- Airbnb/vacation rentals: Permitted if less expensive than approved hotel (receipt required)

DAILY SUBSISTENCE ALLOWANCE (Per Diem):
GCC countries (UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain, Oman):
- Meals: AED 200/day
- Incidentals: AED 50/day

International (Europe, Americas, Asia):
- Meals: AED 350/day
- Incidentals: AED 100/day

Per diem applies when traveling >50km from base city and staying overnight.

GROUND TRANSPORTATION:
- Airport transfers: Taxi/Careem/Uber from airport — reimbursable (receipt required)
- Local transport at destination: AED 100/day taxi allowance
- Rental car: Permitted if cheaper than taxi over trip duration (Economy class car only)
- Mileage for personal car: AED 0.70 per km (approved business travel only)

CLIENT ENTERTAINMENT:
- Purpose: Building and maintaining client relationships
- Meals: Up to AED 300 per person (client must be present)
- Events (sports, shows): Pre-approval from department head required for > AED 500 total
- Always obtain VAT invoice with guest name for proper documentation
- Gifts to clients: See Code of Conduct (max AED 500 per occasion)

ALCOHOL AT BUSINESS EVENTS:
- Company reimburses reasonable alcohol at licensed venues for client entertainment
- Must be consumed legally (licensed venue, not in public)
- Cannot be the majority or sole expense claimed

TRIP RECONCILIATION:
- Submit all travel expenses within 5 days of returning
- Finance Portal → Travel → Trip Reconciliation
- Unused cash advance returned immediately upon return
- Missing receipts: Finance may decline up to 20% of unreceipted claims
