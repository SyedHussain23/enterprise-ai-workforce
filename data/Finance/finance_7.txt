Finance Policy Document

Topic: Internal and External Audit Process

Details:
The company conducts regular audits to ensure financial accuracy, compliance, and risk management.

INTERNAL AUDIT:
- Conducted by: Internal Audit team (independent from Finance)
- Frequency: Quarterly for high-risk areas, annually for standard processes
- Purpose: Verify controls, identify risks, ensure policy compliance, prevent fraud
- Scope: Financial transactions, expense claims, payroll, vendor payments, asset management

WHAT INTERNAL AUDITORS DO:
- Review samples of financial transactions for accuracy and policy compliance
- Verify expense claims have proper supporting documents
- Test that procurement policy (PO requirement, approval thresholds) is being followed
- Check payroll for unauthorized changes or anomalies
- Review access controls to financial systems
- Assess effectiveness of financial controls

YOUR COOPERATION IS REQUIRED:
- If contacted by Internal Audit: Respond promptly (within 2 business days)
- Provide requested documents and information honestly
- Do not delay, alter, or destroy records requested by auditors
- Obstruction of audit: Serious disciplinary offence

EXTERNAL AUDIT:
- Conducted by: Big 4 or top-tier independent audit firm
- Frequency: Annual (financial year-end audit)
- Purpose: Independent verification of financial statements for board/shareholders
- Timing: January–March (for December year-end companies)

EMPLOYEE ROLE IN EXTERNAL AUDIT:
- Auditors may request to interview employees directly
- Finance coordinator notifies relevant employees in advance
- Answer auditor questions honestly and provide requested documents
- Do not make commitments about future business during auditor interviews without Finance Director clearance

FRAUD PREVENTION:
- Segregation of duties: No single employee controls an entire financial process end-to-end
- Dual authorization: Payments above thresholds require two approvers
- System controls: Finance systems automatically flag unusual transactions
- Reporting fraud: Immediately report suspected fraud to ethics@company.com or Internal Audit
- Whistleblower protection: Reporter identity fully protected

AUDIT FINDINGS:
- Findings shared with department heads after audit completion
- Action plans required for all Medium and High findings within 30 days
- Progress tracked quarterly until findings are closed
- Repeated findings treated as serious management issue

ANNUAL FINANCIAL STATEMENTS:
- Audited financial statements completed by 31st March
- Shared with board for approval in Q1 board meeting
- UAE requirement: Financial statements filed with relevant authority
