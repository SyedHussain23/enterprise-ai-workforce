Finance Policy Document

Topic: Capital Expenditure (CAPEX) Policy

Details:
Capital expenditure is investment in long-term assets that provide value beyond one financial year.

WHAT IS CAPEX:
- Purchase of physical assets: Servers, hardware, office equipment, vehicles, fit-out/renovation
- Purchase of intangible assets: Long-term software licenses (>1 year), patents, trademarks
- Development costs: Building a system or product for internal use (if certain criteria met)
- CAPEX is capitalized (spread over asset useful life) vs OPEX (expensed in same period)

CAPEX vs OPEX DISTINCTION:
CAPEX (Capitalize):
- Asset useful life > 1 year
- Cost > AED 5,000 per item
- Provides future economic benefit

OPEX (Expense immediately):
- Recurring cost (monthly/annual subscriptions < 1 year)
- Maintenance and repairs (not improvements)
- Items < AED 5,000 (minor items expensed as OPEX regardless of life)

CAPEX APPROVAL PROCESS:
1. Business case prepared by requesting department (Finance Portal → CAPEX → New Request)
2. Business case includes: Asset description, cost, expected life, ROI/benefit justification, alternatives considered
3. Approval thresholds:
   - Under AED 25,000: Finance Director
   - AED 25,000–100,000: CFO
   - Over AED 100,000: CEO and Board Capital Committee
4. All CAPEX must fit within annual CAPEX budget (approved by Board each January)
5. Unbudgeted CAPEX requires CFO + Board approval regardless of amount

DEPRECIATION (Accounting Impact):
- Finance applies depreciation to all capitalized assets over their useful life
- Standard useful lives used by company:
  * IT equipment: 3 years (33.3% per year)
  * Office furniture: 5 years (20% per year)
  * Vehicles: 4 years (25% per year)
  * Building fit-out: 10 years (10% per year)
  * Software: 3 years (33.3% per year)

ASSET REGISTER:
- All CAPEX items added to Fixed Asset Register maintained by Finance
- Asset tag applied (coordinates with IT Asset Management for IT assets)
- Annual physical asset verification (November)
- Assets no longer in use: Finance must be notified for disposal/write-off process

DISPOSAL AND WRITE-OFF:
- Assets no longer usable: Submit disposal request via Finance Portal → Assets → Disposal Request
- Finance assesses: Market value, book value, disposal method
- Proceeds from asset sale: Credited to company (not department budget)
