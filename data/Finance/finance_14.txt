Finance Policy Document

Topic: Petty Cash and Corporate Card Policy

Details:
Guidelines for managing petty cash and corporate credit card usage.

PETTY CASH:
- Petty cash maintained in each department for small, urgent, miscellaneous expenses
- Each department has a petty cash custodian (designated by department head)
- Petty cash float: AED 500–2,000 per department (amount set by Finance)

PETTY CASH USAGE:
- For expenses under AED 100 where supplier cannot issue proper invoice (taxi, small items, postage)
- Not for: Regular planned purchases, personal items, alcohol, or items over AED 100
- Each withdrawal: Petty cash voucher must be filled in (Finance Portal → Petty Cash → Voucher)
- Receipt attached to every petty cash voucher (no receipt = no reimbursement)

PETTY CASH REPLENISHMENT:
- Custodian submits reconciliation with all vouchers and receipts to Finance
- Finance replenishes float when balance below 25% of original float
- Monthly reconciliation mandatory even if not requesting replenishment
- Finance Portal → Petty Cash → Monthly Reconciliation

CORPORATE CREDIT CARDS:
- Issued to: Grade 5+ employees, frequent business travelers (Grade 3–4 with justification)
- Application: Finance Portal → Corporate Card → Card Request (manager approval required)
- Card limit: AED 5,000–25,000 (based on role, set by Finance on application)
- Card type: Visa Business / Mastercard Business (company Citibank / Emirates NBD commercial cards)

CORPORATE CARD RULES:
1. Card is for BUSINESS USE ONLY — personal expenses are prohibited
2. Never lend card to another person
3. PIN must not be shared — each card is personal
4. Lost/stolen card: Call bank immediately (number on back of card) + notify Finance within 1 hour
5. Card cancelled on: Resignation, termination, or extended leave (>30 days)

MONTHLY CARD RECONCILIATION (Mandatory):
1. Finance Portal → Corporate Cards → Monthly Statement (available by 5th of month)
2. Code each transaction with: Cost center, expense category, project code
3. Upload receipts for ALL transactions (no receipt = personal liability for that transaction)
4. Submit reconciliation by 15th of month for prior month's transactions
5. Finance reviews and posts to accounting system

CONSEQUENCES OF CARD MISUSE:
- Personal expenses on company card: Employee must reimburse within 5 days of notification
- Repeated misuse: Card cancelled, disciplinary action
- Fraudulent use: Immediate termination and potential criminal prosecution
