Finance Policy Document

Topic: Financial Compliance and Anti-Money Laundering Policy

Details:
The company complies with all UAE financial regulations, anti-money laundering (AML) laws, and international financial standards.

UAE FINANCIAL REGULATIONS:
- UAE Anti-Money Laundering Law: Federal Decree-Law No. 20 of 2018
- UAE Combating Terrorism Financing: Federal Decree-Law No. 7 of 2014
- UAE Sanctions: Company complies with UAE Cabinet Decision No. 74 of 2020 (sanctions list)
- FATF Recommendations: UAE is a FATF member — company follows global AML standards

WHAT IS MONEY LAUNDERING:
Money laundering is the process of making illegally obtained funds appear legitimate. It occurs in three stages:
1. Placement: Introducing illegal cash into the financial system
2. Layering: Moving money through complex transactions to hide origin
3. Integration: Returning money to the economy as seemingly legitimate

EMPLOYEE AML OBLIGATIONS:
All employees must:
1. Know Your Customer (KYC): Do not onboard clients without completing KYC verification
2. Report suspicious activity: Report any suspicious transactions or behavior to Compliance Officer
3. No cash transactions: Company does not accept large cash payments (AED 55,000+ threshold per UAE law)
4. No payments to sanctioned parties: Do not process payments to OFAC/UAE sanctioned entities
5. Complete AML training: Annual mandatory training (30 minutes online — compliance team schedules)

RED FLAGS TO REPORT:
- Client requesting unusual payment routing (paying via third party, different country)
- Client unwilling to provide standard identification
- Payments in round numbers without business justification
- Requests to receive payments in personal bank accounts
- Client business does not match apparent income/wealth level
- Unusual cash transactions

REPORTING SUSPICIOUS ACTIVITY:
- Report to: Compliance Officer at compliance@company.com
- Do NOT alert the suspicious party (tip-off is illegal under UAE law)
- Compliance Officer determines if regulatory reporting required (Financial Intelligence Unit — FIU)
- All reports treated confidentially

SANCTIONS COMPLIANCE:
- All new vendors and clients screened against UAE and international sanctions lists
- Finance system automatically checks all payment beneficiaries
- Any match: Payment blocked, Compliance Officer notified immediately
- Do not process payments to blocked or sanctioned parties even if instructed by management

ANTI-BRIBERY (See also HR Code of Conduct):
- Company complies with UAE Anti-Corruption Law
- Report any bribery request (from client, supplier, or government official) immediately to ethics@company.com
- Facilitation payments (small payments to expedite government services): Prohibited
