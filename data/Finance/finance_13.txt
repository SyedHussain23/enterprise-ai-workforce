Finance Policy Document

Topic: Financial Reporting and Management Accounts

Details:
The company produces timely and accurate financial reports to support decision-making.

MONTHLY MANAGEMENT ACCOUNTS:
- Produced by: Finance team
- Deadline: 10th working day of following month
- Recipients: CFO, CEO, Board, Department Heads
- Contents:
  * Income Statement (Profit & Loss)
  * Balance Sheet
  * Cash Flow Statement
  * Budget vs Actual variance analysis (by department)
  * KPI dashboard (revenue, gross margin, operating costs, headcount)

QUARTERLY REPORTS:
- Produced within 15 working days of quarter end
- More detailed than monthly management accounts
- Includes: Detailed P&L analysis, working capital review, capex tracking, headcount analysis
- Board presentation prepared by CFO

ANNUAL FINANCIAL STATEMENTS:
- Full audited financial statements: Completed by March 31st
- Prepared under IFRS (International Financial Reporting Standards)
- Audited by external auditor
- Filed with relevant UAE authorities as required

FINANCIAL REPORTING BY DEPARTMENT HEADS:
- Monthly: Review your department's cost report (sent by Finance by 5th of each month)
- Variance explanation: Required for any line item over 10% variance vs budget
- Submit explanation via Finance Portal → Reports → Variance Explanation
- Deadline for variance commentary: 15th of each month

KPIs TRACKED FOR OPERATIONS:
- Revenue per employee
- Operating cost ratio
- Gross margin %
- Days Sales Outstanding (DSO) — for sales-related departments
- Headcount vs budget

HOW TO ACCESS FINANCIAL REPORTS:
- Finance Portal → Reports → Management Accounts (your department data only, unless you have wider access)
- Full company reports: Available to Grade 6+ and above
- Specific data requests: Email finance-reporting@company.com

CONFIDENTIALITY OF FINANCIAL DATA:
- Financial reports are classified as CONFIDENTIAL (see Data Classification Policy)
- Do not share financial figures with external parties without Finance Director approval
- Do not discuss company financials in public or with competitors
- Financial results are commercially sensitive, especially before public release

BUDGET TRACKER:
- Finance Portal → Reports → My Department Budget: Live view of your department's spend vs budget
- Updated daily with all processed transactions
- Allows managers to monitor spend and take action before overspending
