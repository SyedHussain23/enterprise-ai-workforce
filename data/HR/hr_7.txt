HR Policy Document

Topic: Internal Transfer Policy

Details:
Internal transfers allow employees to move between departments, locations, or roles within the same organization.

ELIGIBILITY FOR INTERNAL TRANSFER:
- Minimum 12 months in current role
- Current role performance rating must be 3 or above (meets expectations)
- No active disciplinary proceedings
- Skills and qualifications must meet requirements of target role
- Transfer subject to mutual agreement between releasing and receiving departments

TYPES OF TRANSFERS:
1. Voluntary Transfer: Employee-initiated, applies via Internal Job Board
2. Management-Initiated Transfer: Business requirement, agreed with employee
3. Rotational Assignment: Temporary cross-functional experience (3–6 months)
4. International Transfer: Transfer to another country (separate policy applies)

VOLUNTARY TRANSFER PROCESS:
Step 1: Employee informs current manager of intent to apply for internal role (courtesy, not permission)
Step 2: Apply via HR Portal → Internal Job Board
Step 3: Recruitment process same as external hire (interview, assessment)
Step 4: Offer made if successful — includes transition timeline
Step 5: Transition period: 4–6 weeks handover with current team
Step 6: New role start date confirmed jointly by both managers

SALARY ON TRANSFER:
- Lateral transfer: Salary maintained at current level (may be adjusted to new grade band minimum)
- Upward transfer (promotion): 15% minimum increase
- Downward transfer: Rare; salary protected for 6 months then adjusted

RESPONSIBILITIES DURING TRANSITION:
- Employee must complete all current project responsibilities before transfer
- Knowledge transfer documentation required for all critical tasks
- Replacement plan agreed before exit from current role
- Access rights updated on first day in new role

INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS:
- Separate relocation policy applies
- Company covers: visa processing, one-way flights for employee and immediate family, temporary housing (up to 60 days), school search assistance for children
- Compensation adjusted to cost of living in destination country
- Assignment agreements signed before transfer

INTERNAL JOB BOARD:
- All open positions posted at: HR Portal → Careers → Internal Openings
- Updated weekly on Mondays
- Application deadline typically 2 weeks from posting
- Priority given to internal candidates over external hires
