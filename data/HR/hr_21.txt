HR Policy Document

Topic: Health and Wellbeing Policy

Details:
The company is committed to the physical, mental, and financial wellbeing of all employees.

MENTAL HEALTH SUPPORT:
- Employee Assistance Program (EAP): 6 free confidential counseling sessions per year
- Access: Contact HR for referral (name not disclosed to employer)
- Topics covered: Stress, burnout, personal relationships, grief, financial anxiety, anxiety
- 24/7 mental health helpline: Call HR hotline for after-hours crisis support

WELLNESS PROGRAMS:
- Monthly wellness sessions (yoga, mindfulness, nutrition talks) — held in office and online
- Annual health screening: Blood pressure, BMI, glucose check — provided free by company at company clinic
- Flu vaccination: Annual free flu shots arranged by HR (October)
- Health and Fitness challenges: Step challenges, wellness weeks with prizes

GYM AND FITNESS:
- Corporate gym membership subsidy: AED 300/month reimbursement
- Submit gym receipt to Finance → Allowances → Gym Reimbursement
- On-site gym available at HQ building (if applicable)

ERGONOMICS AND WORKSPACE:
- All workstations assessed for ergonomic setup on joining
- Ergonomic chairs and adjustable desks available on request
- Submit request via IT Portal → Facilities → Ergonomic Equipment

RAMADAN WELLNESS:
- Healthy iftar and suhoor provided during Ramadan
- Prayer room with proper facilities (ablution area, prayer mats, Qibla direction)
- Flexibility for prayer times maintained throughout the year

DISABILITY AND ACCESSIBILITY:
- Company is committed to inclusive workplace for people with disabilities
- Reasonable adjustments made to workplace, equipment, and working hours
- Contact HR for confidential conversation about accommodations needed

FINANCIAL WELLBEING:
- Annual financial wellness workshop (budgeting, UAE financial planning)
- DEWS/savings information sessions
- Employee access to financial planning resources via HR portal

EMPLOYEE RECOGNITION PROGRAM:
- Employee of the Month: AED 1,000 voucher + recognition certificate
- Team of the Quarter: AED 500/member + lunch with CEO
- Annual Star Awards: Cash prizes and certificates at company gala
- Nominations: Via HR Portal → Recognition → Nominate Colleague

SPORTS AND SOCIAL:
- Annual Team Building Day: Company-funded activity
- Sports teams: Football, cricket, padel — join via HR Portal → Social Clubs
- Monthly social events: Dinners, brunches, cultural celebrations
- Ramadan Iftar and National Day celebrations organized by company
