HR Policy Document

Topic: Maternity and Parental Leave Policy

Details:
The company provides comprehensive parental leave in compliance with UAE Labour Law and beyond minimum requirements.

MATERNITY LEAVE (UAE Labour Law — Federal Decree No. 33 of 2021):
- Total entitlement: 60 calendar days
- First 45 days: Full pay (100% salary)
- Next 15 days: Half pay (50% salary)
- After official leave: Additional 45 days unpaid leave if employee or child is ill (medical certificate required)

COMPANY-ENHANCED MATERNITY LEAVE (Above UAE Law):
- Company provides: 90 days full pay maternity leave
- This exceeds the UAE legal minimum to attract and retain talented employees
- Eligible from Day 1 of employment

WHEN TO NOTIFY:
- Inform HR and manager in writing by the end of the 4th month of pregnancy
- Submit medical certificate confirming pregnancy and expected due date
- Leave start date: Typically 2–4 weeks before expected delivery date (flexible, agreed with manager)

MATERNITY LEAVE PROCESS:
1. Notify manager and HR in writing (email acceptable)
2. Submit medical certificate to HR within 7 days of notification
3. HR creates maternity leave record
4. Manager arranges maternity cover (HR supports with replacement)
5. Employee confirms return date 4 weeks before end of leave
6. Return to work meeting: HR, manager, and employee

BREASTFEEDING RIGHTS (UAE Law):
- For 18 months after birth, female employee entitled to two daily 30-minute breaks for breastfeeding
- Breaks may be combined into one 1-hour break by agreement with manager
- These breaks are paid and not deducted from working hours

PATERNITY LEAVE:
- Male employees: 10 working days paid paternity leave
- Must be taken within 6 months of child's birth
- Apply via HR Portal → My Leaves → Paternity Leave
- Submit birth certificate within 30 days of birth

ADOPTION LEAVE:
- 30 days paid leave for adopting a child (for primary caregiver)
- Documentation: Official adoption papers required

RETURN TO WORK SUPPORT:
- Phased return available: 4 weeks at 80% capacity by agreement
- Flexible hours for first 3 months post-maternity (core hours must be maintained)
- Company provides designated nursing room with privacy and refrigeration

MATERNITY PROTECTION:
- Employees cannot be dismissed during pregnancy or maternity leave (UAE law)
- Performance review postponed until 3 months after return
- Role guaranteed upon return (or equivalent role of same grade)
