HR Policy Document

Topic: Work From Home (WFH) and Remote Work Policy

Details:
The company supports flexible working arrangements while maintaining productivity and team collaboration.

WFH ELIGIBILITY:
- Available to all employees who have completed probation (6 months)
- Role must be suitable for remote work (assessed by manager)
- Employee must maintain the same performance standards as in-office
- WFH is a privilege, not an entitlement — may be revoked if productivity declines

WFH ALLOWANCE:
- Up to 2 days per week WFH for eligible employees
- Maximum 8 WFH days per month
- WFH days must be agreed with manager at start of each week (by Friday for following week)
- WFH on Mondays and Fridays requires explicit approval (to avoid extended weekends)

WFH REQUEST PROCESS:
1. Discuss WFH arrangement with your manager
2. Log WFH days in HR Portal → Attendance → WFH Request
3. Manager approves or rejects in HR system
4. Once approved, WFH days are logged in attendance records

EQUIPMENT AND INTERNET:
- Company provides: Laptop, necessary software licenses
- Employee is responsible for: Stable internet connection, suitable workspace
- Internet allowance: AED 150/month provided to Grade 4+ employees on regular WFH arrangement
- VPN is mandatory for all WFH days (no exceptions)
- Company will not reimburse personal internet bills for ad-hoc WFH

PRODUCTIVITY EXPECTATIONS DURING WFH:
- Available on Microsoft Teams/Slack from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM
- Attend all scheduled meetings via video (camera ON unless explicitly exempt)
- Respond to messages within 30 minutes during core hours (10 AM–3 PM)
- Update task board / project management tool by end of day
- Do not use WFH as substitute for childcare or personal commitments

SECURITY REQUIREMENTS FOR WFH:
- Must connect via company VPN at all times
- Company laptop must have screen lock (auto-lock after 5 minutes)
- Do not use public Wi-Fi without VPN
- No printing of confidential company documents at home
- Family members must not use company laptop

FULL REMOTE WORK (100% Remote):
- Available for specific roles only, by prior agreement in employment contract
- All equipment (monitor, keyboard, ergonomic chair) provided for full-remote roles
- Travel to office required quarterly for team events (expenses covered)

WFH REVOCATION:
- Manager may revoke WFH if: productivity below expectations, policy violations, team attendance required, client requirements change
- Revocation with minimum 2 weeks notice (except in disciplinary cases)
