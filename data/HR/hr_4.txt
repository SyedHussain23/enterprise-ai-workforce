HR Policy Document

Topic: Resignation and Exit Process

Details:
The resignation and exit process is governed by UAE Labour Law Federal Decree No. 33 of 2021 and company policy.

NOTICE PERIOD:
- Standard notice period: 30 calendar days for employees with less than 5 years service
- Employees with 5+ years service: 60 calendar days notice required
- Senior management (Grade 6+): 90 calendar days notice
- Notice period begins from the date HR receives the formal written resignation
- Both employer and employee must respect the notice period
- UAE Labour Law allows mutual agreement to waive or shorten the notice period

RESIGNATION PROCESS:
Step 1: Submit formal resignation letter to direct manager (email acceptable, confirmed hard copy preferred)
Step 2: Manager notifies HR within 24 hours
Step 3: HR acknowledges resignation within 2 business days and confirms last working day
Step 4: Employee must not take annual leave during notice period (unless approved by management)
Step 5: HR schedules exit interview within first week of notice period
Step 6: IT initiates access revocation schedule for last working day
Step 7: Finance calculates final settlement (salary, gratuity, outstanding leave)

EXIT CHECKLIST (must complete before last day):
- Return all company assets: Laptop, phone, access card, parking pass, any company vehicle
- Clear all outstanding approvals and handover all work to successor
- Complete knowledge transfer documentation
- Return any keys, fobs, or physical access cards
- Settle any outstanding company loans or advances
- Exit interview with HR

FINAL SETTLEMENT (UAE Labour Law):
- All dues paid within 14 days of last working day (legal requirement)
- Final salary: Prorated for days worked in last month
- Annual leave encashment: Unused leave (up to maximum allowed) paid out
- End of Service Gratuity: Calculated per UAE Labour Law
- Any deductions: Loans, advance salary, damage to company property

END OF SERVICE GRATUITY:
- 1–5 years service: 21 calendar days basic salary per year
- After 5 years: 30 calendar days basic salary per year
- Maximum gratuity: 2 years total basic salary
- Gratuity is based on BASIC salary only (not housing, transport, or other allowances)
- Employees who resign (vs. terminated) receive full gratuity after 1 year service

VISA CANCELLATION:
- Company processes visa cancellation after last working day
- Employee has 30 days after visa cancellation to leave UAE or change visa status
- HR assists with visa-related documentation

Exit Interview:
- Conducted by HR in confidence
- Feedback used to improve company culture
- Results anonymized and shared with senior leadership quarterly
