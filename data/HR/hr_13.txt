HR Policy Document

Topic: UAE End of Service Gratuity (EOSB)

Details:
End of Service Gratuity (EOSB) is a mandatory benefit under UAE Labour Law Federal Decree No. 33 of 2021 for all private sector employees.

WHO IS ENTITLED:
- All employees on unlimited and limited contracts
- Must have completed at least 1 year of continuous service
- Applies to all nationalities working in the UAE private sector

CALCULATION FORMULA:
For service up to 5 years:
- 21 calendar days of basic salary for each year of service

For service beyond 5 years:
- 30 calendar days of basic salary for each year beyond 5 years
- Maximum cap: 2 years total basic salary

Example Calculation:
- Employee with 3 years service, AED 10,000 basic salary
- Daily rate = AED 10,000 / 30 = AED 333.33
- Gratuity = 21 days × AED 333.33 × 3 years = AED 20,999.79

IMPORTANT RULES:
- Gratuity is calculated on BASIC SALARY only (not housing, transport, or other allowances)
- Gratuity is paid regardless of reason for leaving (resignation, termination, contract end)
- Employee who resigns before completing 1 year: No gratuity
- Employee who resigns after 1 but before 3 years: One-third of full entitlement
- Employee who resigns after 3 but before 5 years: Two-thirds of full entitlement
- Employee who resigns after 5 years: Full entitlement

WORKER PROTECTION SCHEME (DEWS):
- Effective from 1 January 2020 for certain free zones
- Monthly contributions made by employer to DEWS account
- More portable than end-of-service model
- Employee owns the account and can access on leaving

WHEN IS GRATUITY PAID:
- Within 14 days of last working day (UAE Labour Law requirement)
- Paid together with final salary
- Employer who delays gratuity payment may face legal penalties

GRATUITY FOR PART-TIMERS:
- Calculated proportionally based on actual working hours vs full-time hours

TO CALCULATE YOUR GRATUITY:
- Use HR Portal → Payroll → Gratuity Calculator
- Requires: Start date, basic salary, reason for leaving
