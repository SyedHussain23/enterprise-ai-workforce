HR Policy Document

Topic: Training and Development Policy

Details:
The company invests in continuous learning to develop talent and drive organizational growth.

TRAINING BUDGET:
- Annual training budget per employee: AED 5,000–15,000 (based on grade level)
- Grade 1–3: AED 5,000/year
- Grade 4–5: AED 8,000/year
- Grade 6+: AED 15,000/year
- Unused budget does not roll over to next year
- Requests above budget require CHRO approval

MANDATORY TRAINING (All employees):
1. Information Security Awareness Training — Annual (online, 2 hours)
2. Anti-Harassment and Code of Conduct — Annual (online, 1 hour)
3. Data Privacy and GDPR Compliance — Annual (online, 1 hour)
4. Fire Safety and Emergency Procedures — Annual (in-person, 30 minutes)
5. UAE Labour Law Overview — On joining and annual refresh

Mandatory training must be completed within:
- New joiners: First 30 days
- Annual refresh: By 31st December each year
Failure to complete mandatory training: Written warning, access restrictions

TYPES OF TRAINING:
1. On-the-Job Training (OJT): Shadowing, stretch assignments, job rotation
2. Online Learning: LinkedIn Learning, Coursera, Udemy for Business (unlimited access)
3. Internal Workshops: Monthly department-specific sessions, lunch & learns
4. External Training: Conferences, seminars, certified courses
5. Certification Support: Company pays for professional certifications (CIPD, PMP, CFA, etc.)
6. Higher Education Support: Tuition reimbursement for part-time relevant degrees (up to AED 25,000/year)

HOW TO REQUEST TRAINING:
1. Identify training aligned to your IDP (Individual Development Plan)
2. Submit training request via HR Portal → Learning → Request Training
3. Include: Course name, provider, dates, cost, business justification
4. Manager approves, then HR validates budget availability
5. Finance processes payment directly or reimburses employee on receipt

TRAINING BOND:
- For courses costing AED 10,000+, a training bond applies
- Bond period: 12–24 months depending on cost
- If employee resigns within bond period, they repay a pro-rated amount of training cost
- Bond agreement signed before training enrollment

LEARNING MANAGEMENT SYSTEM (LMS):
- Access online training library at: learning.company.internal
- Over 5,000 courses available in English and Arabic
- Completion certificates automatically sent to HR file
- Monthly recommendations based on your role and IDP
