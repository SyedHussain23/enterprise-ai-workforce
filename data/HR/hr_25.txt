HR Policy Document

Topic: HR Support Services and Self-Service Guide

Details:
HR provides a wide range of services to support employees throughout their employment journey.

HR SELF-SERVICE PORTAL:
URL: https://hr.company.internal
Available 24/7 for:
- View and download payslips
- Apply for leave (all types)
- View leave balance
- Submit WFH requests
- Update personal details
- Access company policies
- View payroll history
- Submit grievances
- Access training catalog and enroll in courses
- View organizational chart
- Submit expense claims (linked to Finance portal)

HR TEAM CONTACTS:
- General HR queries: hr@company.com
- Payroll issues: payroll@company.com
- Recruitment: talent@company.com
- Ethics and complaints: ethics@company.com (confidential)
- HR Director (escalations): hrd@company.com
- HR Helpdesk: +971-4-XXX-XXXX (Sunday–Thursday, 8 AM–6 PM)
- Emergency HR support (after hours): +971-55-XXX-XXXX

COMMON HR REQUESTS AND TURNAROUND TIME:
- Leave approval: Manager response within 48 hours
- Salary certificate (for visa/bank): 3 business days
- Experience letter: 5 business days
- Employment verification letter: 3 business days
- NOC (No Objection Certificate): 5 business days
- Payroll queries: Resolved by 20th of month for same-month salary
- Emirates ID assistance: HR coordinates application/renewal with PRO team

SALARY CERTIFICATE:
- Request via HR Portal → My Documents → Request Certificate
- Specify purpose (visa, bank, rental, school enrollment)
- Available in English and Arabic
- Signed and stamped by HR Director

EXPERIENCE LETTERS:
- Issued upon request for current or former employees
- Contains: Employment dates, designation, department, brief duties description
- Formally issued on company letterhead with stamp

PRO SERVICES (Government Relations):
- Visa processing for new joiners
- Visa renewals and cancellations
- Emirates ID applications and renewals
- Labour contract registration
- Contact PRO team via HR Portal → Government Services

MEDICAL INSURANCE SUPPORT:
- New enrollment, addition of dependents: HR processes within 5 days
- Insurance card replacement: HR Portal → My Benefits → Insurance → Request Card
- Pre-approval for specialist referral: Call insurance provider on card
- Network hospital list: HR Portal → My Benefits → Insurance → Hospital Network

FREQUENTLY ASKED HR QUESTIONS:
Q: When is salary credited? A: Last working day of month via WPS
Q: How do I access my payslip? A: HR Portal → Payroll → My Payslips
Q: Can I check my leave balance? A: HR Portal → My Leaves → Leave Balance
Q: How do I add a dependent to insurance? A: Email HR with marriage/birth certificate
Q: How do I get a salary advance? A: HR Portal → Finance → Salary Advance Request
