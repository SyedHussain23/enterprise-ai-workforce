HR Policy Document

Topic: Promotion and Career Development

Details:
The company is committed to employee growth and has a structured promotion framework.

PROMOTION CRITERIA:
- Minimum 12 months in current role before being eligible for promotion
- Consistent performance rating of 4 or 5 in last two annual reviews
- Demonstrated readiness for next level (competency assessment)
- Open position at next grade level or business justification for new role creation
- Manager recommendation and HRBP approval

PROMOTION PROCESS:
1. Manager identifies high-performing employee for promotion
2. Calibration session with peers at the same grade to ensure fairness
3. HR validates against compensation bands and headcount plan
4. Compensation & Benefits team determines new salary
5. CHRO or HR Director approves
6. Offer letter issued with 30-day effective date
7. New reporting structure and responsibilities documented

CAREER GRADE STRUCTURE:
- Grade 1: Junior/Entry Level
- Grade 2: Mid-Level
- Grade 3: Senior Individual Contributor
- Grade 4: Lead / Team Lead
- Grade 5: Manager
- Grade 6: Senior Manager / Associate Director
- Grade 7: Director
- Grade 8: VP / Executive

SALARY ON PROMOTION:
- Minimum 15% salary increase on promotion
- Adjusted to meet new grade salary band minimum
- Housing and transport allowances reviewed and adjusted accordingly

DEVELOPMENT PLANS:
- All employees have an Individual Development Plan (IDP) updated annually
- IDP identifies: skill gaps, training needed, stretch assignments, and career goals
- HR provides access to LinkedIn Learning, Coursera, and internal training library
- Study assistance available: Company funds up to AED 15,000/year for relevant courses or certifications

LATERAL MOVES:
- Employees may request lateral transfers to different departments/roles
- Eligible after 1 year in current role
- Apply via HR Portal → Internal Job Board
- Subject to department head approval

SUCCESSION PLANNING:
- Each department identifies top talent and succession candidates annually
- Succession candidates get priority for promotion and leadership development programs
- High-potential employees enrolled in Fast-Track Leadership Program

MENTORSHIP PROGRAM:
- Available to all employees with 6+ months service
- Matched with senior leader mentor for 12-month program
- Bi-monthly sessions covering: career planning, skill building, leadership
