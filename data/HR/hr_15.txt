HR Policy Document

Topic: UAE Public Holidays and Special Leave

Details:
UAE public holidays are granted in addition to annual leave entitlement as per UAE Federal Government declarations.

UAE PUBLIC HOLIDAYS (Approximate Dates — Official dates confirmed annually by government):
1. New Year's Day — 1 January (1 day)
2. Eid Al Fitr — End of Ramadan (3 days) — dates vary annually based on moon sighting
3. Arafat Day / Eid Al Adha — Dhul Hijja 9-12 (4 days) — dates vary annually
4. Islamic New Year (Hijri New Year) — Muharram 1 (1 day)
5. Prophet Muhammad's Birthday (Mawlid Al Nabawi) — Rabi Al Awwal 12 (1 day)
6. Commemoration Day — 30 November (1 day) — Martyrs' Day
7. UAE National Day — 2–3 December (2 days)

Total UAE Public Holidays: Approximately 13–16 days per year (varies with weekend alignment)

HOLIDAY COMPENSATION:
- If a public holiday falls on a weekend (Friday/Saturday), the following working day off is granted
- Employees required to work on a public holiday receive: 1 day off compensation + 50% pay supplement
- Employees in essential services may have staggered holidays

RAMADAN HOURS:
- Working hours reduced to 6 hours/day for Muslim employees during Ramadan (UAE law requirement)
- Non-Muslim employees: 7 hours/day
- Modified schedules announced by HR at start of Ramadan
- Iftar events and related cultural events may be organized by company

SPECIAL LEAVE (not deducted from annual leave):
1. Marriage Leave: 3 days paid leave for own marriage (once in service)
2. Bereavement Leave (first-degree relative): 5 days
3. Bereavement Leave (other relative): 3 days
4. Hajj Leave: 30 days (once in service, unpaid, for Muslim employees)
5. Study Leave: Per training policy and manager approval
6. Blood Donation: 1 day per donation (maximum 2 per year)
7. Jury Duty / Official Government Summons: Days granted as paid leave with documentation

HOW TO CHECK PUBLIC HOLIDAY SCHEDULE:
- HR Portal → Leave → Holiday Calendar
- Updated each January when government announces official dates
- Email notification sent to all employees at start of each year

COMPANY-SPECIFIC ADDITIONAL HOLIDAYS:
- Founder's Day (Company Anniversary): 1 day additional paid leave
- Year-end close period: Typically last week of December (operational roles maintain coverage)
