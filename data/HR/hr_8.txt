HR Policy Document

Topic: Annual Performance Review and Appraisal Process

Details:
The Annual Performance Review (APR) is the cornerstone of the company's talent management system.

PERFORMANCE REVIEW CYCLE:
- Goal Setting: January–February (set objectives for the year)
- Mid-Year Review: June–July (progress check, goal adjustment)
- Year-End Review: November–December (final assessment)
- Calibration: December (cross-team fairness review)
- Results Communicated: January (salary letters, promotions, development plans)

PERFORMANCE RATING SCALE (1–5):
- 5: Outstanding — Consistently exceeds all expectations, role model for others
- 4: Exceeds Expectations — Frequently goes above requirements, strong contributor
- 3: Meets Expectations — Achieves all goals, reliable and competent performer
- 2: Below Expectations — Partially meets some goals, improvement needed
- 1: Unsatisfactory — Does not meet key objectives, performance plan required

GOAL SETTING (SMART Framework):
- Specific: Clearly defined outcome
- Measurable: Quantifiable metric
- Achievable: Realistic and attainable
- Relevant: Aligned to department and company objectives
- Time-bound: Clear deadline
- Minimum 5, maximum 10 objectives per employee
- Goals entered in HR Portal → Performance → My Goals

HOW THE REVIEW WORKS:
1. Employee completes self-assessment (rating themselves on each goal + behavioural competencies)
2. Manager completes independent rating
3. 1:1 review meeting between employee and manager (minimum 60 minutes)
4. Calibration with peer managers (manager presents, team agrees on final ratings)
5. Final rating shared with employee by HR letter
6. Employee signs acknowledgement (can add comments/objections)

PERFORMANCE IMPROVEMENT PLAN (PIP):
- Triggered for employees rated 1 or 2
- Duration: 60–90 days
- Specific measurable targets set with weekly check-ins
- HR and manager co-own the PIP
- Successful completion: Employee retains job, revisited in 6 months
- Unsuccessful completion: Role separation (with legal compliance)

BEHAVIOURAL COMPETENCIES ASSESSED:
- Collaboration and Teamwork
- Communication and Stakeholder Management
- Innovation and Problem Solving
- Leadership and Accountability
- Customer Focus (external/internal)
- UAE Cultural Awareness and Inclusion

APPEALING YOUR RATING:
- Employees may formally appeal within 14 days of rating notification
- Submit appeal to HR Partner with written justification
- HR facilitates review between employee and manager + HR Director
- Appeal outcome is final
