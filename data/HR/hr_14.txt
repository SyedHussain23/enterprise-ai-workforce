HR Policy Document

Topic: Code of Conduct and Employee Behavior Policy

Details:
The Code of Conduct defines the ethical and behavioral standards all employees are expected to uphold.

CORE PRINCIPLES:
1. Integrity: Act honestly and transparently in all business dealings
2. Respect: Treat all colleagues, clients, and stakeholders with dignity
3. Accountability: Take responsibility for actions and decisions
4. Confidentiality: Protect company and client information
5. Compliance: Follow all laws, regulations, and company policies

PROFESSIONAL CONDUCT:
- Treat all colleagues with respect regardless of rank, nationality, gender, or religion
- Use professional and constructive language in all workplace communications
- Avoid gossip, rumor spreading, or behavior that damages team morale
- Dress appropriately (see Dress Code Policy)
- Maintain punctuality for meetings and deliverables
- Represent the company positively in all external interactions

CONFLICTS OF INTEREST:
- Disclose any personal relationship with a vendor, supplier, or competitor
- Do not make business decisions that benefit yourself or close family members personally
- Do not work for a competitor or client outside company hours without approval
- Annual conflict of interest declaration required from all employees (November)

SOCIAL MEDIA POLICY:
- Do not post negative content about the company, colleagues, or clients
- Do not share confidential information on social media
- Personal opinions expressed online must not imply company endorsement
- Company accounts managed only by authorized Marketing team

GIFTS AND HOSPITALITY:
- Gifts up to AED 200 in value may be accepted
- Gifts above AED 200 must be declared to HR within 5 days and may be declined or donated
- Do not accept gifts from vendors in exchange for business decisions
- Company gifts to clients: Maximum AED 500 per occasion, Finance approval required

ANTI-BRIBERY AND CORRUPTION:
- Zero tolerance policy for bribery
- Never offer or accept money, gifts, or favors to influence business decisions
- Report any suspicion of bribery to Ethics Hotline: ethics@company.com or anonymously via Ethics Portal
- Whistleblower protection guaranteed

DATA AND INFORMATION HANDLING:
- Never share client or employee personal data with unauthorized parties
- Lock your screen when leaving your desk
- Do not use personal USB drives on company computers
- Follow clean desk policy: No sensitive documents left visible on desk

Violations reported to: ethics@company.com | HR Director
