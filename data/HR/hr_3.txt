HR Policy Document

Topic: Employee Onboarding Process

Details:
The onboarding process ensures every new employee is set up for success from Day 1. The process spans the first 90 days of employment.

PRE-JOINING (1 week before start date):
- HR sends welcome email with joining instructions, parking info, and dress code
- IT provisions laptop, email account, and system accesses
- Line manager prepares onboarding schedule and buddy assignment
- Employee receives digital copy of employee handbook and policies to review

DAY 1 — Welcome and Orientation:
- 8:00 AM: Report to HR reception (Building A, Ground Floor)
- HR completes all documentation: Employment contract signing, visa paperwork (if applicable), bank account setup form
- Required documents to bring: Original passport, Emirates ID (or application receipt), educational certificates, previous employer experience letters, passport-sized photographs (4 copies)
- IT provides laptop, access badge, and system credentials
- Tour of office facilities: cafeteria, prayer rooms, parking, emergency exits
- Meet HR partner and line manager

WEEK 1 — Company Introduction:
- Company overview: Mission, vision, values, organizational structure
- Introduction to all department heads
- Meet your team members
- HR portal training: How to apply for leave, update details, access payslips
- Benefits enrollment: Health insurance, life insurance, transportation
- IT orientation: Email setup, VPN, software tools, security policy briefing
- Compliance training: Code of conduct, anti-harassment policy, data privacy

30-DAY CHECK-IN:
- 1:1 meeting with HR to address concerns
- Manager feedback session on initial performance
- Confirm probation objectives are clear
- Salary and benefits confirmation

60-DAY MILESTONE:
- Mid-probation review with manager
- Training needs assessment
- Role clarity and target setting

90-DAY COMPLETION:
- Formal probation review
- Performance objectives set for full year
- Employee moves to confirmed status (if probation passed)
- Full benefits activation including annual leave accrual eligibility

Buddy Program:
- Each new joiner is assigned a buddy from their team (peer, not manager)
- Buddy is the go-to person for informal questions for the first 30 days
- Buddy check-ins: Day 3, Day 14, Day 30

HR Portal Self-Service Setup:
- Access HR portal at: https://hr.company.internal
- Complete profile: Personal details, emergency contact, bank details
- Enroll in benefits within first 14 days
