HR Policy Document

Topic: Recruitment and Hiring Process

Details:
The company follows a structured, merit-based hiring process to attract and select the best talent.

RECRUITMENT PHILOSOPHY:
- Merit-based selection: Best qualified candidate for the role
- Internal-first: All vacancies posted internally before external advertising
- Diversity-focused: Interview panels include diverse members, job descriptions are gender-neutral
- Emiratization: UAE Nationals given priority consideration as per government requirements

INTERNAL JOB POSTING:
- All vacancies posted on HR Portal → Internal Careers
- Posted for minimum 7 days before external advertising
- All employees with 12+ months service may apply
- Hiring manager must interview all qualified internal applicants

EXTERNAL RECRUITMENT CHANNELS:
- LinkedIn, Bayt, GulfTalent for UAE/GCC roles
- Direct sourcing by Talent Acquisition team
- Preferred recruitment agencies: Pre-approved vendor list (HR manages)

SELECTION PROCESS:
Stage 1: CV screening by Talent Acquisition team
Stage 2: HR screening call (30 minutes — culture fit, expectations, notice period)
Stage 3: Technical interview with hiring manager (60 minutes)
Stage 4: Panel interview with department head and cross-functional member (60 minutes)
Stage 5: For senior roles (Grade 5+): CEO/MD interview
Stage 6: Reference checks (2 professional references required)
Stage 7: Offer and onboarding

OFFER PROCESS:
- Verbal offer made by Talent Acquisition
- Written offer letter sent via DocuSign within 48 hours of verbal offer
- Acceptance deadline: 3 business days (may request extension)
- Offer subject to: Reference checks, educational verification, background check (for certain roles)
- Medical test: Required for UAE visa processing — arranged by HR

REFERENCE CHECKS:
- 2 professional references required (direct managers from previous employers)
- Personal references not accepted
- Reference check call conducted by HR before final offer confirmed
- Any discrepancy between information provided and references: Offer may be withdrawn

BACKGROUND AND CREDENTIALS VERIFICATION:
- Educational certificates verified via official institutions
- Employment history verified via references and LinkedIn
- Criminal background check: For roles involving finance, children, or security clearance

CANDIDATE EXPERIENCE:
- All candidates receive response within 5 business days of application
- Rejected candidates receive brief feedback if requested
- Internal candidates: Must be told outcome within 5 days of final interview
