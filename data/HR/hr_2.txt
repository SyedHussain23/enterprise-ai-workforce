HR Policy Document

Topic: Attendance and Working Hours Policy

Details:
Standard working hours are 9 hours per day (8 hours work + 1 hour break) for 5 days per week (Monday to Friday), totaling 45 hours per week.

During Ramadan:
- Working hours are reduced to 6 hours per day for all Muslim employees as per UAE Labour Law
- Non-Muslim employees: 7 hours per day during Ramadan month
- Modified schedules communicated by HR at the start of Ramadan

Attendance Tracking:
- All employees must clock in and out using the biometric attendance system at the office entrance
- Remote/WFH employees log attendance via the HR portal digital check-in
- Attendance records reviewed monthly by HR
- Three or more unexplained absences in a month triggers a formal warning

Late Arrival Policy:
- Up to 15 minutes late: Recorded as late (3 occurrences per month allowed before deduction)
- 15–60 minutes late: Half-hour deduction from salary
- More than 60 minutes late without prior approval: Half-day leave deducted
- Repeated lateness (more than 5 times per month): Formal disciplinary action

Absence Without Leave (AWOL):
- One day AWOL: Written warning + unpaid day deduction
- Three consecutive days AWOL: Final warning
- Seven consecutive days AWOL: Termination under UAE Labour Law Article 44 (No notice, no gratuity)

Overtime Policy:
- Overtime must be pre-approved by direct manager in writing
- Standard overtime rate: 1.25x normal hourly rate
- Friday/public holiday overtime: 1.5x normal hourly rate
- Maximum overtime: 2 hours per day / 120 hours per year (UAE law)
- Overtime payment processed in the following month's payroll

Flexi-Time Arrangements:
- Available to Grade 4+ employees with manager approval
- Core hours: 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM must be maintained
- Start time can vary between 7:00 AM – 10:00 AM
- Must complete full 9-hour day

Remote Work (Work From Home):
- Eligible employees may WFH up to 2 days per week
- Requires manager approval and documented in HR portal
- WFH days cannot be Monday or Friday without special approval
- Employee must be reachable during core hours
