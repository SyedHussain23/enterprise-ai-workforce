HR Policy Document

Topic: Employee Benefits and Compensation Package

Details:
The company offers a comprehensive benefits package designed for UAE-based employees.

HEALTH AND MEDICAL:
- Company-paid group medical insurance for employee + spouse + up to 3 children
- Insurance class: Enhanced plan (AED 500,000 annual limit per person)
- Coverage includes: Outpatient, inpatient, emergency, maternity, dental, optical (limited)
- Insurance provider: Premium network (Daman/AXA)
- Pre-approval process for specialist referrals and surgeries
- International emergency coverage included

LIFE AND DISABILITY INSURANCE:
- Life insurance: 3x annual basic salary (death benefit, paid to beneficiary)
- Total permanent disability: 2x annual basic salary
- Accidental death and disability: Additional 2x annual basic salary
- Enrollment automatic from Day 1 (beneficiary form must be completed in first 30 days)

TRANSPORTATION:
- Transport allowance: AED 1,500–3,500/month (grade dependent)
- Subsidized parking at office building (limited spaces — first come, allocated)
- Company car: Grade 6+ only, per company car policy

ANNUAL LEAVE: 21 days (30 days after 5 years) — see Leave Policy HR_1

ANNUAL FLIGHT TICKET ALLOWANCE:
- One round-trip economy ticket per year to home country for employee
- Grade 5+: Business class for flights over 6 hours
- Family (spouse + children): Economy tickets provided for Grade 5+
- Amount: Actual cost or cap of AED 3,000 per person (whichever is lower)
- Must be used in the calendar year, does not carry forward

EDUCATION ALLOWANCE (for dependent children):
- Available from Grade 5+
- Up to AED 25,000 per child per year (maximum 3 children)
- For schools in UAE or internationally if on international assignment
- Submit fee invoice to Finance by September

HOUSING ALLOWANCE:
- Included in monthly salary package: AED 3,000–12,000/month (grade dependent)

MOBILE PHONE AND COMMUNICATIONS:
- Company SIM: Grade 4+ eligible, AED 200/month credit
- Personal mobile plan reimbursement: Up to AED 150/month for all employees (on business calls)

EMPLOYEE DISCOUNTS AND PERKS:
- Gym/fitness club membership subsidy: AED 300/month
- Corporate discounts at partner hotels, restaurants, and retail brands
- Free tea, coffee, and light snacks in office
- Annual team building event funded by company

RETIREMENT (DEWS):
- Monthly employer contribution to DEWS (Dubai Employee Workplace Savings) account
- Contribution: 5.83% of monthly basic salary (if in DIFC or applicable free zones)
- Portable fund — employee retains on leaving
