HR Policy Document

Topic: Payroll and Salary Structure

Details:
The company salary structure and payroll process ensures accurate and timely compensation for all employees.

SALARY COMPONENTS:
- Basic Salary: 40–60% of total package (used for gratuity and leave encashment calculations)
- Housing Allowance: 20–30% of total package (UAE standard practice)
- Transport Allowance: 10–15% of total package
- Other Allowances: Communication, education (if applicable), role-specific allowances

PAYROLL CALENDAR:
- Salary payment date: Last working day of each month (UAE WPS — Wage Protection System)
- All salaries transferred via WPS through company bank account
- Payslips available on HR portal by 25th of each month
- Any salary queries must be raised by 20th of the month to process corrections

SALARY REVIEW CYCLE:
- Annual salary review: Conducted in Q1 (January–March) each year
- Increment effective from: 1st April annually
- Merit increases based on performance rating:
  * Rating 5 (Outstanding): 15–20% increase
  * Rating 4 (Exceeds Expectations): 10–15% increase
  * Rating 3 (Meets Expectations): 5–8% increase
  * Rating 2 (Below Expectations): 0–3% increase (with performance improvement plan)
  * Rating 1 (Unsatisfactory): No increment, performance management initiated

HOW TO ACCESS PAYSLIPS:
1. Login to HR Portal → Payroll → My Payslips
2. Download PDF for any month
3. Year-end salary certificates available by 31st January

SALARY ADVANCE:
- Employees may request salary advance up to 50% of monthly basic salary
- Available after completing 6 months of service
- Maximum one advance per 6-month period
- Advance repaid in equal installments over next 3 months
- Request via: HR Portal → Finance → Salary Advance Request

SALARY DEDUCTIONS:
- Absence/unpaid leave
- Late arrival (per policy)
- Salary advance repayment
- Company loan repayments
- UAE: No income tax deductions (UAE has no personal income tax)
- DEWS (Workplace Savings Scheme) contributions if enrolled

WAGE PROTECTION SYSTEM (WPS):
- UAE law requires all private sector salaries paid via WPS
- Salary must be transferred within 10 days of the agreed salary date
- Company complies fully with WPS requirements

Bank Account Requirements:
- Salary transfer to UAE bank account only
- Employee must provide IBAN within first week of joining
- Approved banks: Emirates NBD, FAB, ADCB, Dubai Islamic Bank, Mashreq, RAK Bank
- Bank account setup assistance available from HR
