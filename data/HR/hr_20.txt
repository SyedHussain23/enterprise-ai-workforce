HR Policy Document

Topic: Employee Records and Data Privacy

Details:
The company manages employee personal data in accordance with UAE data protection laws and company policy.

EMPLOYEE DATA COLLECTED:
- Personal: Full name, date of birth, nationality, marital status, contact details
- Employment: Start date, grade, role, salary, performance records
- Legal: Passport number, Emirates ID, visa details, work permit
- Financial: Bank account, salary history
- Medical: General health (required for insurance enrollment, not diagnostic)
- Emergency contacts: Name and phone number of 2 emergency contacts

DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY:
- All employee records stored in encrypted HR Information System (HRIS)
- Physical records locked in HR secure storage
- Access restricted to: HR team, direct manager (role-relevant only), Finance (salary data)
- IT security audit of HRIS conducted annually
- Data backed up daily with 90-day retention

ACCESSING YOUR OWN DATA:
- Employees have right to view all personal data held by company
- Request data access via HR Portal → My Profile → View My Data
- Response within 5 business days
- Right to correct inaccurate data at any time (update personal details via HR Portal)

WHAT EMPLOYEES CAN UPDATE THEMSELVES:
Via HR Portal → My Profile:
- Contact number and personal email
- Emergency contact details
- Bank account details (with verification)
- Home address
- Marital status and number of dependents

DATA THAT REQUIRES HR TO UPDATE:
- Name change (legal document required)
- Nationality change
- Emirates ID update
- Salary changes
- Promotion/grade changes

DATA RETENTION:
- Active employees: Data held for duration of employment
- After leaving: Data retained for 7 years (UAE legal requirement for employment records)
- After 7 years: Data deleted or anonymized

EMPLOYEE DATA NOT SHARED WITH THIRD PARTIES EXCEPT:
- UAE government authorities (Ministry of Human Resources, immigration) — as legally required
- Medical insurance provider (for policy enrollment)
- Payroll bank (for salary transfer — minimum data only)
- Legal counsel in case of disputes

GDPR / DATA PRIVACY:
- Company complies with UAE Federal Law No. 45 of 2021 on Personal Data Protection
- Data Protection Officer (DPO) contact: dpo@company.com
- Any data breach affecting employee data reported to DPO within 24 hours

DOCUMENT COPIES REQUIRED ON JOINING:
- Passport (validity minimum 6 months from joining date)
- Emirates ID or receipt of application
- Previous employment letters
- Educational certificates
