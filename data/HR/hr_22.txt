HR Policy Document

Topic: Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI) Policy

Details:
The company celebrates diversity and is committed to creating an inclusive workplace for people of all backgrounds.

COMMITMENT TO DIVERSITY:
The company employs people from over 40+ nationalities in line with our UAE/GCC location. We believe diversity of thought, culture, and background makes us stronger and more innovative.

PROTECTED CHARACTERISTICS (No discrimination on basis of):
- Gender / gender identity
- Age
- Nationality and ethnicity
- Religion
- Marital or family status
- Pregnancy or maternity
- Disability (physical or mental)
- Sexual orientation (where applicable under local law)

INCLUSIVE WORKPLACE PRACTICES:
1. Inclusive recruitment: Job descriptions use neutral language, interview panels include diverse members
2. Equal pay: Annual pay equity review ensures no gender or nationality pay gap
3. Inclusive meetings: Meetings scheduled during core hours; not during prayer times or religious holidays
4. Language: English is official language but translation support available
5. Religious accommodation: Prayer rooms provided, time for prayers during day granted
6. Flexible holidays: Employees may swap public holidays for religious observances from own faith (with HR approval)

GENDER EQUALITY:
- Target: 40% women in management roles by 2027
- Women in Leadership Program: Annual cohort of 20 women, 12-month development program
- Maternity protection: Role guaranteed on return from maternity leave
- Pay equity: Salaries benchmarked annually by external consultant to ensure gender parity

NATIONALITY AND EMIRATIZATION:
- Company committed to UAE Emiratization targets as per Ministry of Human Resources requirements
- UAE National Development Program: Dedicated development plans for Emirati employees
- Priority consideration for UAE Nationals in qualifying roles
- Mentor matching: Senior Emirati mentors for junior UAE National employees

DISABILITY INCLUSION:
- Physical accessibility: Wheelchair ramps, accessible restrooms, elevator access
- Digital accessibility: HR systems comply with WCAG 2.1 accessibility standards
- Reasonable adjustments: Work schedule, equipment, workspace modifications available
- Confidential disclosure encouraged: No disadvantage for disclosing a disability

REPORTING DISCRIMINATION:
- Report immediately to HR Partner or via ethics@company.com
- Anonymous reporting: Ethics Portal (link on company intranet)
- Zero tolerance: All discrimination investigated within 10 business days

DEI Committee:
- Cross-functional team driving inclusion initiatives
- Monthly internal events celebrating cultural milestones
- Annual DEI report published internally
