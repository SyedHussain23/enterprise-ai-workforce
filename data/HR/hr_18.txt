HR Policy Document

Topic: Probation Policy

Details:
All new employees serve a probation period during which both parties evaluate suitability for the role.

PROBATION PERIOD:
- Standard probation: 6 months (UAE Labour Law maximum)
- May be extended in exceptional cases with HR approval
- Extension communicated in writing before original probation end date
- During probation, employee is entitled to all statutory benefits (health insurance, etc.)

NOTICE DURING PROBATION:
- Either party may terminate during probation with 14 days written notice (UAE law minimum)
- Company policy: 30 days notice from employer during probation
- Employee giving notice: 14 days (per UAE law)
- Immediate termination for gross misconduct (no notice required)

PROBATION REVIEW PROCESS:
- 30-day check-in: Informal review with manager, verbal feedback
- 90-day formal review: Manager completes probation review form, submitted to HR
- Final probation review (Day 150–180): Pass/Fail/Extend decision made
- Employee receives written confirmation of probation outcome within 5 days of final review

OBJECTIVES DURING PROBATION:
- Minimum 3–5 SMART objectives agreed within first 2 weeks of joining
- Objectives cover: Technical skills, behavioral competencies, knowledge areas
- Progress reviewed monthly by manager

SUCCESSFUL COMPLETION:
- Employee receives confirmation letter
- Full benefits activate (annual leave accrual eligible to use, bonus eligibility)
- Annual leave taken during probation counts toward entitlement
- Performance rating starts from confirmation date

PROBATION EXTENSION:
- Can be extended by up to 3 additional months (total maximum 6 months under UAE law)
- Employee informed in writing with specific areas requiring improvement
- Dedicated coaching/support provided during extension

UNSUCCESSFUL PROBATION:
- 14 days notice given (or payment in lieu)
- No gratuity payable (less than 1 year service)
- Work permit and visa cancellation processed by HR
- No internal job board applications during probation unless pre-approved

LEAVE DURING PROBATION:
- Sick leave: Permitted after completing 3 months, with medical certificate
- Annual leave: Accrued but generally not taken during probation (exceptional cases considered)
- Emergency leave: Granted at manager discretion
